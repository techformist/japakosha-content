godaavaryaa maahaanadyaa uttare sinhaparvate |
supunye maahurapure sarvatiirthasamanvite || 1 ||

jajjne'treraanasuuyaayaam pradoshe budhavaasare |
maargaashiirshyaa mahaayoogii dattaatreyo digambarah || 2 ||

maalaam kundiim ca damarum shuulam shankham sudarshanam |
dadhaanah shadbhuhaistraigmaa yogamargapravartakah || 3 ||

bhasmoddulitasarvaango jataajuutaviraajitah |
rudraakshabhushitanutah shaambaviimudrayaa yutah || 4 ||

bhaktaanugrahakrunityam paapataapaartibhajanah |
baalonmattapishaachaabhah smartrugaamii dayaanidhih || 5 ||

yasyaasti maahure nividraa nivaasah sinhaparvate |
praatah snaanam ca gangaayaam dhyaanam gandharvattane || 6 ||

kurukshetre caacamano dhuutaapaeshware tathaa |
vibhutidhaaranan praatahsandhyaa ca karahaaatake || 7 ||

kolaapure'sya bhikshaa ca paancaale'pi ca bhojanam |
dinago vishthalapure tungaapaanam dine dine || 8 ||

puraanashravanam yasya naranaaraayanaashrame |
vishraamo sarade saayamsandhyaa pashchimasaagare || 9 ||

kaartaveeryaarjunaayaadaayogardhimubhayiim prabhu |
svaamatattvam ca yadave bahuguruaaptamuttamam || 10 ||

aanveekshiimalarkaaya prahlaadaaya tagiiyate |
aayuraajaaaya ca varaan saadhyebhyo mokshasaadhanam || 11 ||

mantraamsha vishnudattaaya somakaantaa ya karma ca |
sa evaavirabhuudbhuuh poornaarnavasamiiptah || 12 ||

bhaadre maasi site pakse caturthyaam rajavipratah |
sumatyaa praaksindhutiire ramye peethaapure vare || 13 ||

ya aacaaravyavahitipraayashcittopadeshakrit |
nijaagrajavaamdhapanguu vilokya pravrajan sudhiih || 14 ||

maatapritormude drishtiim gatim taabhyaamupaanayat |
mahiim pradakshiiniikritya gokarne tryabdaamaavasan || 15 ||

tatah krishnaatam praapya martukaamaam saputrakaam |
nivartya braahmaniiimamando pradosham vratamaadishat || 16 ||

tatputram vibudham kritva tasyaa janmaantare prabhu |
putro bhuutvaa naraharinaamako desha uttare || 17 ||

kaancane nagare'pyambaanayadvipado vibhuh |
maasi paushe site pakse dvitiyaayaa shaneridine || 18 ||

jaatamaatro'pi cunkaaram papaadhaathaapi mookavat |
saphaabdaan lelayaa sthitva naanaakautukakritah prabhu || 19 ||

upaniito'pathavedaan saptame vatsare svayam |
aashvaasya janiinam putradvayadaanena bodhatah || 20 ||

kaashiim gatvaa'shhtaangayogaabhyaasi krishnasarasvatiiim |
kritvaam gurum yatirbhuutvaa vedaarthaan samprakaashya ca || 21 ||

luptasannyaasidhram ca tena turyaashramam bhuvi |
merum pradakshiiniikritya shishyaan kritvaa'pi bhuurishah || 22 ||

pitrubhyaam darshanam dadvaa dvijam shuularujaaruditam |
kritvaa'naamayamaashvaasya saayan devam maahamatim || 23 ||

abdam sthitvaa vaidyanathakshetre krishnaatatata tatah |
billavaatyaam caturmaasaan vibhurgatvatah agratah || 24 ||

nrusinhavaatikaakshetre dvaadaashaabdaan vasan sudhiih |
tatra sthitvaa'pi gandharvaparametyaavasan mathe || 25 ||

jiivayitvaa mrutaan dughdvaa vandyaa ca mahishiim harih |
vishvaruupam darashayitva yatayei vishvanaatakah || 26 ||

bahviiraamanushiirleelaa kritvaa gupto'pi tatra ca |
ya aste bhagavaan dattah so'smaa rakshatu sarvadaa || 27 ||

yaa saptavimshatishlokaih kritaa nakshatramaalikaa |
tadbhaktaebhyo'rpitaa bhaktaabhinaashriidattatushtaye || 28 ||

dvaadaashyaamaashvine krishne shriipaadasyotsavah mahaan |
maaghe krishne pratipadi narasimha prabhostathaa || 29 ||
