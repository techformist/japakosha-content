shri vasishthauvacha |
atha varshasahreina tam pitamah ayyayau |
darunam hi tapah siddhyai vishagnirapishitalah || 1 ||

manasaiva pranamyainam sa tathaiva sthita sati |
ko varah kushchamayamiti chintanvitabhavat || 2 ||

asmrutam prarthasyeham varamemekamimam vibhum |
anayasii chayasyii syamaham jeevasu chikaa || 3 ||

asyoktmya dvividha su chirbhutva lakshya vishyamahi |
praninam saha sarvesham hrdayam surabhiratha || 4 ||

yathabhihamametena graseyam sakalam jagat |
kramena kshu dvina shaya kshu dvina shah param sukham || 5 ||

iti sanchimayantima uvacha kamalalayah |
anyadri shyasatha drishtva sthitaabhra ravopamam || 6 ||

brahmuvacha |
putri karkatike rakshakulashailabhr malike |
utthisthtva tva tu tushto smi gruhanabhimatam varam || 7 ||

karkatyuvacha |
bhagavan bhutabhaveshya syamaham jeevasu chikaa |
anayasii chayasyi vidhe rupayasi chedvaram || 8 ||

shrivasishthauvacha |
evamstvaiti tukatva punaraha pitamahah |
su chikaa sopasargaa tvam bhavishyasi vishu chikaa || 9 ||

su kshmya ma yaa sarvalokahim saa karishyasi |
durbojanaa durarambhaa murkhaah dushtitayashcha ye || 10 ||

durdeshavasinaa dush tashtehim hinsa karishyasi |
pravi shya aahrdayam pra naih padmaplihaa badhanaat || 11 ||

vaatalekaatmika vyadhirbhavishyasi vishu chikaa |
sagunam vigunam chaiva janama saadayishyasi || 12 ||

gunanvitachiktsaartham mantro tu maya uchyate
brahmuvacha |
himadre ruttare pa rshve karkatie naama ra kashii |
vishu chikaa bhidhanaa sa naa mnaapya baadhi ka |

tasya mantrah |
om hrim hram rim ram vishnushaktaye namah |
om namo bhagavati vishnushaktimena om hara hara naya naya pacha pacha matha matha
utsa daya utsa daya da ure kuru svaha himavantam gaccha jeeva sah sah sah chandramandala gato asi svaha |
iti mantrimaha mantaram nyasavaamakarodare |
maarjayedaaturaakaaram tena haste samyutah || 14 ||

himashailabhimukhyena vidruta tam vichintayet |
karkatie karkasha kra amda mantaram udgaradar ditaam || 15 ||

aaturam chintayechandre rasayanahirdihistitam |
ajaraamaramam yuktamuktam sarvadhibhibhramaih || 16 ||

saadhako hi shuchirbhutvaa sva cha antah susa manhitah |
kramen anena sakalam prouch chinnati vishu chikaam || 17 ||

iti gaganagatastrilokanathah |
gaganagasiddhagrihita siddhamantrah |
gata upagata shakravand yamano |
nija puramakshayama yam ujjvala shrih || 18 ||
