namo brahmaNya-devaaya go-braahmaNa-hitaaya ca |
    jagaddhitaaya kRuShNaaya goviidaaya namo namaH || 1 ||

kiirtanaM shraavaNaM daanaM darshanaM chaapi paarthiva |
    gaavaaM prashyate viira sarvapaapaharaM shivam || 2 ||

ghRRita-kshiira-pradaa gaavo ghRRitayonyo ghRRitodbhavaaH |
    ghRRitanaadyo ghRRitaavartaastaM etaM santu sadaa gRRihe || 3 ||

ghRRitaM me hRidaye nityaM ghRRitaM naabhyaam pratiShTitam |
    ghRRitaM sarveShu gaatreShu ghRRitaM me manasisthitam || 4 ||

gaavo mamaa-agrato nityaM gaavaH pRRiShTata eva ca |
    gaavo me sarvataH chaiva gaavaaM madhyevasaamyaham || 5 ||

ityaachamya japet-saayaM praatah ca puruShassadaa |
    yadahnaatkurutepaapam tasmaat sa parimucyate || 6 ||

## English Translation

(1) Salutations to the God of Brahmins, who is benevolent to cows and Brahmins,
    Salutations to Krishna, who benefits the world, to the Lord of cows, again and again salutations.

(2) O King, singing, hearing, giving, and even seeing cows
    Is indeed proclaimed to remove all sins—this is auspicious.

(3) Cows who give ghee and milk, who are full of ghee, born from ghee,
    May these ghee-born cows always be in my house.

(4) May ghee be in my heart always, may ghee be established at my navel,
    May ghee be in all my limbs, may ghee be established in my mind.

(5) May cows always be before me, may cows always be behind me,
    May cows be all around me, may I always be amidst cows.

(6) Having said this, one should chant in the evening and always in the morning,
    Whatever sin is committed during the day, from that one is freed.
