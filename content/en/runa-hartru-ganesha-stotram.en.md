|| atha stotram |
srishtyaadau brahmanaa samyakpujitah phasiddhaye |
sadaa pa rvatii putrah runanasham karoto me || 1 ||

tripurasya vadhaatpooram shambhunaa samyagarchitah |
sadaa pa rvatii putrah runanasham karoto me || 2 ||

hiranyakaship vaadaanaam vadhaarthe vishnuaarchitah |
sadaa pa rvatii putrah runanasham karoto me || 3 ||

mahishasya vadhe devyaa gananaathah prapoojitah |
sadaa pa rvatii putrah runanasham karoto me || 4 ||

taarakasya vadhaatpooram kumarena prapoojitah |
sadaa pa rvatii putrah runanasham karoto me || 5 ||

bhaaskarena ganesho hi pujitashhavisiddhaye |
sadaa pa rvatii putrah runanasham karoto me || 6 ||

shashinaa kantivurdhyartham pujito gananaayah |
sadaa pa rvatii putrah runanasham karoto me || 7 ||

pa lanaaya svatapasaa vishvaamitrena poojitah |
sadaa pa rvatii putrah runanasham karoto me || 8 ||

idam runaharastotram teevadaaridryanashanam |
ekavaaram pathaen-nityam varshamekam samaahitah || 9 ||

daaridryaaddaaruunaanmuktah kuberasampadam vrajet |
phadamto'yam mahaamantraah saarthapancadashaaksharah || 10 ||

om ganesha runam chindi varenyam hum namah phat |
imam mantram pathedaante tashca shucibhaavanah || 11 ||

ekavi matisamkhyaa bhih purashcaranamiiritam |
sahaaravartanaata samyak shanmaasam priyataam vrajet || 12 ||

bruhaspatisamo jnaane dhane dhanapatirhavet |
asyaivaayutasamkhyaa bhih purashcaranamiiritam || 13 ||

lakshamaavartanaata samyagvaanchitam phalaapnuyaat |
bhuutapretechaa naa naashanam smrutimaatratah || 14 ||

|| atha prayogah |
asya shree runahart ganapatistotra mahaamantrasya |
sadaa shiva rushih
karany aasah |
om ganesha angushthaabhyaam namah |
om runam chindi tarjaneebhyaam namah |
om varenyam madhyamaabhyaam namah |
om hum anaamikaabhyaam namah |
om namah kanishthikaabhyaam namah |
om phat karatalakaraprusht abhyaam namah
shadangany aasah |
om ganesha hridayaaya namah |
om runam chindi shi ras e svaha |
om varenyam shi khaayai vashat |
om hum kavachaaya hum |
om namah netratrayaaya vushat |
om phat astraaya phat
dhyaanam -
sinduuravarnam dvibhujam ganesham
lambodaram padmadale nivishtam |
brahamaadidevaih parisevyamaanam
siddhai ryutam tan pranamaami devam |
lamityaadi panchapoojaa |
|| mantrah ||
