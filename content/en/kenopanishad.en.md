|| ShAnti pAThaH ||

OM apyAyantu mamA~NgAni vAkpRANa- chakShuH shrotR-amatho balam-indriyANi cha sarvANi |
    sarvaM brahma-upanishaM mAham- brahma nirAkuryAm mA mA brahma
nirAkarod-anirAkaraNam-astvanirAkaraNam me'stu |
    tadAtmani nirate ya upanishatsu dharmAste mayi sAntu te mayi santu
OM shAntiH shAntiH shAntiH |
    || praThama khaNdaH |
    OM keneShitaM patati pReshitaM manaH |
kena prANaH praThamaH pRaiti yuktaH |
    keneShitAm vAchamimAM vadanti |
chakShuH shrotR-aM ko u devo yunakti || 1||

shrotR-asya shrotRaM manaso mano ya-
    -dvAcho ha vAchaM sa u prANasya prANaH |
    chakShuShachakShur-atimucya dhIrAH |
    pretyAsmAllokAdAmRutA bhavanti || 2||

na tatra chakShur-gacChati na vAg-gacChati no mano na vidmo na vijAnImo yathaitad-anuShyAdanyada eva tadvitAd-atho aviditA-dadha |
    iti shushruma pUrvAM ye nasta dvyAchachakShire || 3||

yadvAchA-nabhyuditaM yena vA gAbhyudyate |
    tadeva brahma tvaM viddha nedadaM yadidaM upAsate || 4||

yanmanasA na manute yenAhuH manomatamaM |
    tadeva brahma tvaM viddha nedadaM yadidaM upAsate || 5||

yachchakShuShA na pashyati yena chakShURi-gaMsh-i pashyati |
    tadeva brahma tvaM viddha nedadaM yadidaM upAsate || 6||

yachchhrotR-eNa na shRnoti yena shrotRam-idagM shRutamaM |
    tadeva brahma tvaM viddha nedadaM yadidaM upAsate || 7||

yatprANeNa na prANiti yena prANaH praNIyate |
    tadeva brahma tvaM viddha nedadaM yadidaM upAsate || 8||

|| dvitIyaH khaNdaH |
    yadi manyase suvedeti dabhramevApi
nUnaM tva vettha brahmaNa rUpamaM |
    yadasya tvaM yadasya deveShvathanu
mImAMsAyameva te manye viditamaM || 1||

nAham manyase suvedeti no nA vedeti vedacha |
    yo nasta da veda tavedA no na vedeti vedacha || 2||

yasyA mataM tasya mataM matam yasya na veda saH |
    avijnAtaM vijAnAtAm vijnAtaM-avijAnAtAmaM || 3||

pratibodhaviditaM mataM-amRtatvaM hi vindate |
    AtmanA vindate vIryaM vidayA vindate'AmRutamaM || 4||

iha chedA vedIdatha satyamasti |
    na chedihA vedIdaM-mahIti vinaShTiH |
    bhUteShu bhUteShu vichitya dhIrAH |
    pretyAsmAllokAdAmRutA bhavanti || 5||

|| tRitIyaH khaNdaH |
    brahma ha devebhyo vijigyA tasya ha brahmaNa
vijaye devA AmahIyanta || 1||

ta AiXshAMtAsmAkaM evAyaM vijayo'smAkaM evAyaM mahImeti |
    taddhaiShAM vijagyA tebhyaH ha prAdurbabHUva tana vya jAnAta
kimidaM yaxamiti || 2||

te'Agni-mabruvAjAtaveda etadvijAnIhi |
    kimetada yaxamiti tathA || 3||
