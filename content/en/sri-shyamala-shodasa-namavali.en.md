## English Translation

Om salutations to Her who is the yogini of music
Om salutations to the dark-complexioned one
Om salutations to Shyamala
Om salutations to the mistress of mantras
Om salutations to the minister
Om salutations to Her who is in the assembly of the Lord
Om salutations to the supreme mistress
Om salutations to Her who is dear to Shuka
Om salutations to She who plays the veena
Om salutations to the skilled musician
Om salutations to She who holds mudras
Om salutations to She who is dear to the beloved
Om salutations to She who is dear to the lotus
Om salutations to the goddess of the Kadamba tree
Om salutations to She who dwells in the Kadamba forest
Om salutations to She who always grants maddening delight
