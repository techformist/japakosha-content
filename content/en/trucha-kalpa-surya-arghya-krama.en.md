achamya | prananamya | deshakalau samkritlya | ganapati pujam kritva |

sankalpah –
purvokta evam gunavisheshana vishishtayam shubhithau shruti smriti puranokta phalapratyartham shrisav itrisuryanarayana prit yartham bhavi shyottarapuranokta tricharghapurvakaprasanarghyanani cha karishye |

dhyamam –
dhyeyah sada savitrimandalamadhya var ti
narayanah sarasijasanasan nivi shhtah |
keyuravan makarakundalavan kirite
hari hiranyamayapurdhrutashankhachakrah ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hram udyamnadya mitramaha hram om | mitraya namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 1 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hrim arohamnut taramdivam hrim om | ravaye namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 2 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hrum hrdrogam mama surya hrum om | suryaya namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 3 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hrim harimanam cha nashaya hrim om | bhanave namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 4 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hraum shukeshu me harimanam hraum om | khagaya namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 5 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hrah ropanakasu dadhmasi hrah om | pushne namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 6 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hram atho hari draveshu me hram om | hiranyagarbhaya namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 7 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hrim harimanam nidadhmasi hrim om | marichaye namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 8 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hrum udagadyamadityah hrum om | adityaya namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 9 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hrim vishven sahas saha hrim om | savitre namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 10 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hraum dvishantam mahyam randhayamn hraum om | arkaya namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 11 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hrah mo aham dvishite radham hrah om | bhaskaraya namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 12 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hram hrim udyamnadya mitramaha arohamnut taramdivam hram hrim om | mitraravibhyam namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 13 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hrum haim hrdrogam mama surya harimanam cha nashaya hrum hrim om | suryabhanubhyam namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 14 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hraum hrah shukeshu me harimanam ropanakasu dadhmasi hraum hrah om | khagapushbhyam namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 15 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hram hrim atho hari draveshu me harimanam ni dadhmasi hram hrim om | hiranyagarbhamarichibhyam namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 16 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hrum hrim udagadyamadityo vishven sahas saha hrum hrim om | adityasavitribhyam namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 17 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hraum hrah dvishantam mahyam randhayamn mo aham dvishite radham hraum hrah om | arkabhaskarabhyam namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 18 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hram hrim hrum hrim udyamnadya mitramaha arohamnut taramdivam | hrdrogam mama surya harimanam cha nashaya | hram hrim hrum hrim om | mitraravisuryabhanubhyo namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 19 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hraum hrah hram hrim shukeshu me harimanam ropanakasu dadhmasi | atho hari draveshu me harimanam ni dadhmasi | hraum hrah hram hrim om | khagapushahiranyagarbhamarichibhyo namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 20 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hrum hrim hraum hrah udagadyamadityo vishven sahas saha | dvishantam mahyam randhayamn mo aham dvishite radham | hrum hrim hraum hrah om | adityasavitrarkabhaskarebhyo namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 21 ||

om bhurbhuvassuvah | om hram hrim hrum hrim hraum hrah hram hrim hrum hrim hraum hrah
udyamnadya mitramaha arohamnut taramdivam |
hrdogam mama surya harimanam cha nashaya |
shukeshu me harimanam ropanakasu dadhmasi |
atho hari draveshu me harimanam ni dadhmasi |
udagadyamadityo vishven sahas saha |
dvishantam mahyam randhayamn mo aham dvishite radham |
hram hrim hrum hrim hraum hrah hram hrim hrum hrim hraum hrah om | mitra ravi surya bhanu khaga pusha hiranyagarbha marichyadityasavitrark bhaskarebhyo namah |
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah idamarghyam samarpayami || 22, 23, 24 || (iti trih) |
——
prarthana prasanarghyapradanam –
om agnimile purohitam yajñasya devamritvijam | hotaram ratnadhatamam ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah rigvedatmane prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 1 ||

om ishe tvorje tva vayavah sthopayavah stha devo vah savita praprayatu shreshthatamaya karmana apayadhvamaghaniya devabhagamurjasvateeh payasvateeh prajavateehanameeva ayakshmah ma vah stena ieshata magashagamso rudrasya hetih parivo vrunaktu dhruva asmin gopauta syat bahvirayamanasya pashun pahi ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah yajurvedatmane prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 2 ||

om agna a yahi vityate grunahno havyadataayi | ni hauta sati barhishi ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah samavedatmane prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 3 ||

om sham no deveerabhishtay apo bhavantu piteyei | sham yorabhishravantu nah ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah atharvavedatmane prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 4 ||

om saha navavatu | saha nau bhunaktu | saha viryam karavavahai |
tej asvinavadhitamastu ma vidvishavahai ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah upanishadatmane prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 5 ||

om a satyena rajasa vartamano niveshayamnmritam martyancha |
hiranyayena savita rathena a devo yati bhuvana vipashyan ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 6 ||

om udutyam jatavedasam devam vahanti ketavah | drishe vishvaya suryam ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 7 ||

om udvyam tamasparijyotihspashantottaram |
devam devatra suryamagaman jyotiruttamam ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 8 ||

om chitram devananudagananeehakam chakshurmitrasya varunasya agneh |
apradhyavaprithiviantariksham surya atma jagatastasthushcha ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 9 ||

om hamsah shucishadvasantarikashsadhotavedishadatithirdurnasat |
nrishadavarasahitritasadvyasomadasadbhagojaha ritajaa adrijaa ritam ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 10 ||

om savita pashchatatsavita purastatsavitottarata tatsavita dharata tatsavita |
savita nah savatu sarvatatigam savita no rasatam dirghamayuh ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 11 ||

om adityo va esha etanmandalam tapati | tatra ta ruchstadricha mandalangam sa ruchanam lokotha ya esha etasminmandale rchirdipyate tani samani sa samnanam lokotha ya esha etasminmandale rchishi purusasthanii yajugunshi sa yajusha mandalangam sa yajusham lokah | saisha trayeva vidya tapati ya eshontaraditye hiranyamayah purushah ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 12 ||

om adityo vai tejo ojo balam yashah chakshuh shrotram atma manomanyurmanurmrityuh |
satyo mitro vayurakashah prano lokapalah kah kim katam tasya satyamanamamritto |
jeevo vishah katah svayambhu brahmetadamritam eshah purushah eshah bhutanamadhipatih brahmanah | sayujyagam salokatamapnotyeta saamam |
devatanagam sayujyagam sarshitagam samanalokatamapnoti ya evam vedetyupanishat ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 13 ||

om ghruhinah surya adityo na prabha vatyaksharam | madhu ksharanti tadrasm |
satyam vai tadrasmapo jyoteeh rasomritam brahma bhurbhuvassuvarom ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 14 ||

om bha skaraya vidmahe mahaddtyutikaraya dhima hi | tannah adityah prachodayat ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 15 ||

om adityayavidmahe sahasrakaraya dhima hi | tannah suryah prachodayat ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 16 ||

om hamsa hamsayavidmahe paramahamsaya dhima hi | tannah hamsah prachodayat ||
shrisav itrisuryanarayanaparabrahmane namah prarthana prasanarghyam samarpayami || 17 ||

anena tricharghapurvakaprarthana prasanarghyapradanaishcha bhagavan sarvatmakah shri padmini usha chhaya sameta shrisav itrisuryanarayana supr itah suprasanobhavantu ||
