nirvikaram nirakaram nirajanamanamayam |
adyantarahitam purnam brahmaivaham na samshayah || 1 ||

nishkalankam nirabhasam triparicchedavarjitam |
anandamajamavyaktam brahmaivaham na samshayah || 2 ||

nirvisham nirakaram nityam muktaam avikriyam |
prajanaikarasam satyam brahmaivaham na samshayah || 3 ||

shuddham buddham svata siddham param pratyagakhan ditam |
svaprakam parakasham brahmaivaham na samshayah || 4 ||

susukshmama stitamatram nirvikalpam mahattamam |
kevalam paramadvaitam brahmaivaham na samshayah || 5 ||
