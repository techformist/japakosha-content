brahmovacha
stavanam samavedoktam suryasya vyadhimocanam
sarvapapaharam saram dhanarogyakaram param || 1 ||

tam brahma paramam dham jyotirupam sanatanam
tvamaham stotumicchami bhaktanugrahakaram || 2 ||

trailokyalocanam lokanatham papavimocanam
tapasam phaladaram dukhadam pabinam sada || 3 ||

karmanurupaphaladam karmabijam dayanidhim
karmarupam kriyarupam arupam karmabijakam || 4 ||

brahmavishnumaheshanamashcha trigunatmakam
vyadimdam vyadiharam shokamohabhayapaham
sukhadam mokshadam saram bhaktidam sarvakamadam || 5 ||

sarveshvaram sarvarupam saksinam sarvakarmanam
pratyaksam sarvalokanamaprathyaksam manoharam || 6 ||

shashvadarashaham paschadrashadam sarvasiddhidam
siddhisvarupam siddishesh siddhanam paramam gurum || 7 ||

stavarajamidam proktam guhyadguhyataram param
trisandhyam yah pahen nityam vyadhibhyah sa pramucyate || 8 ||

andhyam kushtam cha daridryam rogah shoko bhayah kali |
tasya nashyati vishvesha shrisuryakripaya dhruvam || 9 ||

mahakushti cha galito cakshurhino mahavarni |
yakshtagrasto mahashuli nanavyadiyuto 'pi va || 10 ||

masam krutva havishyam shrutvato mucyate dhruvam |
snanam cha sarvatirthanam labhate natra samshayah || 11 ||

## English Translation

Brahma said:
The hymn told in the Sama Veda, which removes the diseases of Surya,
The essence that removes all sins, bestowing wealth and health, the supreme. || 1 ||

That Brahman, the supreme abode, the form of light, the eternal one,
I wish to praise you, who cause grace to devotees. || 2 ||

The seer of the three worlds, the lord of the worlds, the remover of sins,
The giver of fruits of asceticism, the abode of sorrow for the wicked always. || 3 ||

The giver of fruits according to actions, the seed of action, the treasure of compassion,
The form of action, the form of ritual, the formless, the seed of action. || 4 ||

Possessing the aspects of Brahma, Vishnu, and Maheshwara, the embodiment of three gunas,
The giver of disease, the remover of disease, the remover of delusion, attachment, and fear,
The giver of happiness, the giver of liberation, the essence, the giver of devotion, the fulfiller of all desires. || 5 ||

The lord of all, the form of all, the witness of all actions,
Manifest to all the worlds, unmanifest to others, pleasing. || 6 ||

Always giving taste, giving taste afterwards, giving all Siddhis,
The form of Siddhi, the lord of Siddhas, the supreme guru of the accomplished. || 7 ||

This hymn called Stavaraja has been told, more secret than the secret, the supreme,
Whoever reads it at the three twilights daily is freed from diseases. || 8 ||

Blindness, leprosy, poverty, disease, sorrow, fear, and Kali,
All these certainly perish by the grace of Sri Surya, O Lord of the universe. || 9 ||

The great leper, the one with swollen throat, the one without eyes, the great wound,
The one consumed by tuberculosis, the one with great pain, even though afflicted with many diseases, || 10 ||

Having made a fire offering for a month and having heard this, one is certainly freed,
One obtains the bathing of all holy waters; there is no doubt in this. || 11 ||
