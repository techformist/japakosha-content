## Sankalpam (Resolution)

gururbrahma gururvishnuh gururdevo maheshvarah |
gurussaakshaat param brahma tasmai shri gurave namah |

mama upaatta samasta durita kshayadvara mama manasasankalpa siddhyartham shri sitaramachandra anugraha siddhyartham shrilmadvalmiki ramayanantargate sundarakande ____ sarga shloka parayanam karishye |

## Shri Rama Prarthana (Prayer to Shri Rama)

ramaya ramabhadraya ramachandraya vedase |
raghunathaya nathaya sitayah pataye namah |

ramam lakshmanapurvam raghuvaram sitapatim sundaram |
kakutshtam karunararnavam gunanidhim viprapriyam dharmikam |
rajendram satyasandham dasharathatanayam shyamalam shantamurtim |
vande lokabhiramam raghukulatilakam raghavam ravanarim |
apadamapahartara data sarvasampadan |
lokabhiramam shriramam bhuyah bhuyoh namamyaham |

## Shri Anjaneya Prarthana (Prayer to Shri Hanuman)

goshpadikritavarashim mashikritarakshasam |
ramayanamahamalaratnam vande nilatmajam |

manojavam marutatulyavegam |
jitendriyam buddhimatam varishtham |
vatatmjam vanarayoothamukhyam |
shriramadutam sharanam prapadye |

buddhirbalam yasho dhairyam nirbhayatvam arogita |
ajadyam vakpatutvam cha hanumat smaranaat bhavet |

## Shri Valmiki Prarthana (Prayer to Shri Valmiki)

koojantam rama rameti madhuram madhuraksharam |
aruhya kavitashakham vande valmikikokilam |
