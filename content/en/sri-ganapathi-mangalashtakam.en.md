gajaanaanaaya gaangeyasahajaa sadaaatmanei |
    gauriipriyatanoojaa gaaneshhaayaastu mangalam || 1 ||

naagayajnopaviitaaya navavighninaashinei |
    nandyaadigananaathaa naayakaayaastu mangalam || 2 ||

ibhavaxtraaya chaendraadivanditaa chidaatmanei |
    iishaanapremaapaatraaya cheshtadaayaastu mangalam || 3 ||

sumukhaaya sushundaagrokshiptaamritaghataaya ca |
    surabrunanishishyaaya sukhadaayaastu mangalam || 4 ||

caturbhuaaya chandraardhavilasanmastakaa ya ca |
    caranaavanataanartha taaraaNaayaastu mangalam || 5 ||

vakratuNdaaya vatakei vandyaaya varadaaya ya |
    viiruupaakshasutaayaastu vighnanaashaya mangalam || 6 ||

pramodaamodarupaaya siddhivijaanaaru piiNE |
    prakrishapaapanaashaa phaladaayaastu mangalam || 7 ||

mangalam gananaathaa mangalam harasuunavei |
    mangalam vighnarajaa vighnahartreistu mangalam || 8 ||

shlokaaashatakam idam punyam mangalapradamaadaaraata |
    pathitavyam prayatnena sarvavighanavirittayei || 9 ||
