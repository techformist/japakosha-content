shriiyaH kaaryasiddhiirdhiyaH satsukhardheiH


patiim sajjanaanaam gatim devataanaam || 1 ||


    niyaantaramantaH svayam bhaasamaanam


bhajei vighNaraajam bhavaaniitanuujam || 1 ||





gaNaanaamadhiishaam guNaanaam sadiiSham


kariindraananam kRuttakadarapamaanam || 2 ||


    caturbaahayukta chidaanandasaktaM


bhajei vighNaraajam bhavaaniitanuujam || 2 ||





jagatpraanaviiryam janatraaNaashaur yam


suraabhiishTakaaryam sadaa'kShobhya dhairyam || 3 ||


    guNiishlaaghyacharyam gaNaadhiishavaryam


bhajei vighNaraajam bhavaaniitanuujam || 3 ||





caladavakratsuNDam caturbaahudandam


madasravigaNDam milachandrakhaNDam || 4 ||


    kanadada ntakaaNDam munitraaNaashauNDam


bhajei vighNaraajam bhavaaniitanuujam || 4 ||





nirastaantarayam paridhvastamaayam


chidaanandakaayam sadaa matsahaayam || 5 ||


    ajasraanapaayam tvajam chaaprameiyam


bhajei vighNaraajam bhavaaniitanuujam || 5 ||





avaraam chaabhayam paashapu staakShasuutram


sRuNiim biijapuuram karaih pankajam ca || 6 ||


    dadhaanam saarojaasanaam shaktiyuktaM


bhajei vighNaraajam bhavaaniitanuujam || 6 ||





mahaamuuShaakaaruuDamaadhaarashaktyaa


samaaraadhitaaMghrim mahaamaatRukaabhiH || 7 ||


    samaavRuitya samseivitam devataabhiH


bhajei vighNaraajam bhavaaniitanuujam || 7 ||





shruTiinaam shirobhiH stutam sarvashaktam


patiim siddhibuddhyorgatibhuusuuraaNaam || 8 ||


    suraaNaam variishTham gaNaanaamadhiisham


bhajei vighNaraajam bhavaaniitanuujam || 8 ||





gaNaadhiishasaamraajyasimhaasanastham


samaaraadhyamabjaasanaadyaiH samastaiH || 9 ||


    phaNabhRutsamaba dhatuNDam prasannam


bhajei vighNaraajam bhavaaniitanuujam || 9 ||





lasannaagakayuuramjiirahaaram


bhujangaadhiiraajasphuratkarNapuuram || 10 ||


    kanadbhuutirudraaShararNaadibhuuSham


bhajei vighNaraajam bhavaaniitanuujam || 10 ||





sphuradvyaaghraCharmottariiyopadhaanam


turiiyaadvayaatmaanusaMdhAna dhuryam || 11 ||


    tapoyogivaryam kRupodaaracharyam


bhajei vighNaraajam bhavaaniitanuujam || 11 ||





nijajyotiShaa dyoyayantam samastam


divi jyotiShaaMa maNDalam ca atmanaa ca || 12 ||


    bhajadbhaktasau bhaagyasiddhyarthabiijam


bhajei vighNaraajam bhavaaniitanuujam || 12 ||





sadaavasakalyaaNapurayaa nivaasam


guroraajNayaa kurvataa bhuisureNa || 13 ||


    mahaa yogiivelNaaDasiddhaantinaa ya-


-tkRitam stotramiShTaarthadamatpathadvam || 13 ||
