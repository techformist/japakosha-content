|| shrii-durgAyai namaḥ ||

shlokaḥ
kaushikena viracitaṃ prabodha-bodhakaṃ |
durgā-devyāḥ suprabhātaṃ śṛṇu nipuṇā |
prātar-utthāya yo nityaṃ paṭhet-suprabhātam |
durgā-devī-prasādāt prāpnoti śivasya padam ||

kātyāyanyai namo nityaṃ yā sarvabhūta-hitaiṣiṇī |
durgāstra-bhṛtāṃ deveśī bhadrakālyai namo namaḥ || 1 ||

śaraṇāgata-dīnārta-paritrāṇa-parāyaṇe |
sarvasya durita-hartre devi durge namo'stu te || 2 ||

ambiketi durgeti bhadrakālyeti ca |
caṇḍiketi caṇḍamuṇḍeti bhairavyeti namo namaḥ || 3 ||

mahishāsuramardhinyai śumbha-nishumbha-hantryai |
raktabījāya namastubhyaṃ mahishāsuranāśinī || 4 ||

laliteti rajanīti ramāyai namo namaḥ |
kālarātryai mahārātryai śūlinyai namo namaḥ || 5 ||

trinetrai trishūlāyai trishaktiai namo namaḥ |
triguṇātmikāyai tubhyaṃ mahādevyai namo namaḥ || 6 ||

siṃhavāhinyai chandinyai vṛishavāhyai namo namaḥ |
gaṇeśvarī mahālakṣmyai sarasvatyai namo namaḥ || 7 ||

ādi-shakti-svarūpāyai prakṛityai namo namaḥ |
māyāyai vidyāyai caiva namastubhyaṃ namo namaḥ || 8 ||

pārvatyai haimavatyai ca girijāyai namo namaḥ |
śailajāyai dakṣa-yajñe virodhinyai namo namaḥ || 9 ||

kāmākṣyai kāmeśvaryai ca kāntyai namo namaḥ |
shivāyi shiva-kāmyai ca shiva-dūtyai namo namaḥ || 10 ||

annapūrṇāyai devyai dhanyai dhānyai namo namaḥ |
iṣṭa-pūrti-dāyai caiva bhoga-dāyai namo namaḥ || 11 ||

sarvamaṅgala-maṅgalyai sarva-siddhi-pradāyinī |
sarvapāpa-hare devi mahādevī namo'stu te || 12 ||

śāntiḥ pāthaḥ
oṃ sarvebhyaḥ namaḥ |
shrii-durgā-suprabhātaṃ sampūrṇam ||

## English Translation

(Invocation) Listen carefully, O wise ones, to the awakening hymn
    Of Goddess Durga, composed by Sage Kushika,
    Which awakens spiritual knowledge.
    Whoever reads this Suprabhata daily upon rising at dawn,
    By the grace of Goddess Durga, attains the abode of Shiva.

(1) Salutations, always, to Katyayani,
    Who wishes well for all beings,
    The Goddess of all who wield the Durga missile,
    Salutations, salutations to Bhadrakali.

(2) O Goddess, who are the refuge for those who seek protection,
    Who are the ultimate savior of the oppressed and suffering,
    O Durga, remover of all miseries of everyone,
    Salutations be to You.

(3) Salutations to Her who is called Ambika, Durga, and Bhadrakali,
    Salutations to Her who is called Chandika and Chamunda,
    Salutations, salutations to Bhairavi.

(4) Salutations to Her who slew the buffalo demon Mahisha,
    Who killed Shumbha and Nishumbha,
    Salutations to You, O slayer of Raktabija,
    O destroyer of Mahishasura.

(5) Salutations, salutations to Lalita, Rajani, and Rama,
    Salutations, salutations to Kalaratri and Mahanatri,
    Salutations, salutations to Her who wields the spear.

(6) Salutations, salutations to Her who has three eyes,
    Salutations, salutations to Her who wields the trident,
    Salutations to the three-powered Goddess,
    Salutations, salutations to Great Goddess who embodies the three gunas.

(7) Salutations, salutations to Her who rides a lion,
    Salutations to Chandika, salutations to Her who rides a bull,
    Salutations to Ganeshvari, Mahalakshmi,
    Salutations, salutations to Saraswati.

(8) Salutations, salutations to the primordial Shakti,
    Salutations, salutations to Prakriti (Nature),
    Salutations to Maya, knowledge,
    Salutations, salutations to You.

(9) Salutations, salutations to Parvati, Haimavati,
    Salutations, salutations to Girija,
    Salutations to Shailaja, who opposed Daksha's sacrifice.

(10) Salutations, salutations to Kamakshi, Kameshwari,
    Salutations, salutations to the beautiful one,
    Salutations to Shiva, Shiva-kami,
    Salutations, salutations to Shiva's messenger.

(11) Salutations, salutations to Goddess Annapoorna,
    Salutations, salutations to the wealthy and prosperous one,
    Salutations to the bestower of fulfilled desires,
    Salutations, salutations to the bestower of enjoyment.

(12) O Goddess, auspicious among all auspicious ones,
    Bestower of all accomplishments,
    Remover of all sins,
    O Great Goddess, salutations be to You.

(Peace Prayer) Om, salutations to all beings.
    The Shri Durga Suprabhata is complete.
