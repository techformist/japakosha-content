|| Shri Durgayai Namah ||

**Invocation Verse**
Listen carefully, O wise ones, to the awakening hymn
Of Goddess Durga, composed by Sage Kushika,
Which awakens spiritual knowledge.
Whoever reads this Suprabhata daily upon rising at dawn,
By the grace of Goddess Durga, attains the abode of Shiva.

**Verse 1**
Salutations, always, to Katyayani,
Who wishes well for all beings,
The Goddess of all who wield the Durga missile,
Salutations, salutations to Bhadrakali. || 1 ||

**Verse 2**
O Goddess, who are the refuge for those who seek protection,
Who are the ultimate savior of the oppressed and suffering,
O Durga, remover of all miseries of everyone,
Salutations be to You. || 2 ||

**Verse 3**
Salutations to Her who is called Ambika, Durga, and Bhadrakali,
Salutations to Her who is called Chandika and Chamunda,
Salutations, salutations to Bhairavi. || 3 ||

**Verse 4**
Salutations to Her who slew the buffalo demon Mahisha,
Who killed Shumbha and Nishumbha,
Salutations to You, O slayer of Raktabija,
O destroyer of Mahishasura. || 4 ||

**Verse 5**
Salutations, salutations to Lalita, Rajani, and Rama,
Salutations, salutations to Kalaratri and Mahanatri,
Salutations, salutations to Her who wields the spear. || 5 ||

**Verse 6**
Salutations, salutations to Her who has three eyes,
Salutations, salutations to Her who wields the trident,
Salutations to the three-powered Goddess,
Salutations, salutations to Great Goddess who embodies the three gunas. || 6 ||

**Verse 7**
Salutations, salutations to Her who rides a lion,
Salutations to Chandika, salutations to Her who rides a bull,
Salutations to Ganeshvari, Mahalakshmi,
Salutations, salutations to Saraswati. || 7 ||

**Verse 8**
Salutations, salutations to the primordial Shakti,
Salutations, salutations to Prakriti (Nature),
Salutations to Maya, knowledge,
Salutations, salutations to You. || 8 ||

**Verse 9**
Salutations, salutations to Parvati, Haimavati,
Salutations, salutations to Girija,
Salutations to Shailaja, who opposed Daksha's sacrifice. || 9 ||

**Verse 10**
Salutations, salutations to Kamakshi, Kameshwari,
Salutations, salutations to the beautiful one,
Salutations to Shiva, Shiva-kami,
Salutations, salutations to Shiva's messenger. || 10 ||

**Verse 11**
Salutations, salutations to Goddess Annapoorna,
Salutations, salutations to the wealthy and prosperous one,
Salutations to the bestower of fulfilled desires,
Salutations, salutations to the bestower of enjoyment. || 11 ||

**Verse 12**
O Goddess, auspicious among all auspicious ones,
Bestower of all accomplishments,
Remover of all sins,
O Great Goddess, salutations be to You. || 12 ||

**Peace Prayer**
Om, salutations to all beings.
The Shri Durga Suprabhata is complete.
