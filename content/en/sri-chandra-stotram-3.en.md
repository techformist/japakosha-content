Dhyana -
shvetaambharojjvalatanu sitamaalyagandham |
shvetaashvayuktarathagam surasevitaamghrim || 1 ||
    dorbhyaam dhrutaabhayagadam varadam sudhaamshum
shrivatsamauktikadharan pranamaami chandram || 1 ||

aagneyabhaage saratho dashaashva-
-shchaatreyajo yaamunadeshajashcha || 2 ||
    pratyangmukhashtha caturashrapeethe
gadaadharo no'vatu rohiniishah || 2 ||

Atha stotram -
chandram namaami varadam shankarasya vibhushanam |
    kalaanidhiim kaantiirupam keyuuramakutojjvalam || 3 ||

varadam vandyacharanam vaasudevasya locanam |
    vasudhaahlaadanakaram vidhum tam pranamaamyaham || 4 ||

shvetamaalyaambaradharan shvetagandhaanulepanam |
    shvetchattroullasanmaulim shashinam pranamaamyaham || 5 ||

sarvam jagajjivayasi sudhaarasamayaih karaih |
    soma dehi mamaarogyam sudhaapuritamandalam || 6 ||

raajaatvaam braahmananaam ca ramayaa api sodarah |
    raajaa naathashchaoushiidhiinaam raksha maam rajaniikarah || 7 ||

shankarasya shiroratnam shaarnginashca locanam |
    taarakaanaamadheesastvam taarayaasmaanmahaapadah || 8 ||

kalyaanamuurte varada karunaarasavaaridhe |
    kalashodadhisanjaatakalaanaatha kripaam kuru || 9 ||

kshiiraarnavasamudbuuta cintaamanisahodbhava |
    kaamitaarthaan pradehi tvam kalpadrumasahodarah || 10 ||

shvetaambarah shvetavibhushaaadhyo
gadaadharah shetarucirdvibaahuh || 11 ||
    chandrah sudhaatmaa varadah kiriitei
shreyaamsi mahyam pradadaatu devah || 11 ||

idam nishaakarastotram yah pateh pratyaham narah |
    upadravaat sa mucyeta naatra kaaryaa vichaaraanaa || 12 ||
