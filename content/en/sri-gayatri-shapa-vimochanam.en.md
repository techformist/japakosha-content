1. brahma shApavimochanaM –
asya ShrIbrahmashApavimochana mantrasya nigrahAnugrahakarTA prajApatiRRiShiH, kAmadhugA yatRRI chandaH, bhuktimuktipradA brahmashApavimochanI gA yatrI shaktiH, savitA devatA, brahmashApavimochanArthe jape vinIyogaH |
    mantraM –
saviturBrahmo metyupAsanAtta dbrahmavido vidustAM prayaM ti dhIrAH |
    sumanasA vAchA mamAgrataH ||

OM devI gA yatrI tvaM brahmashApAdvimuktA bhava | (iti trivAraM paThet)

## English Translation

1. Brahma Curse Release –
For this Sri Brahma Curse Release Mantra, Prajapati is the rishi who controls and grants favors, Kamadhuk is the meter, the Brahma-curse-releasing Gayatri Shakti bestowing enjoyment and liberation, Savita is the deity, for release from Brahma's curse the application is japa.

Mantra –
Knowers of Brah meditate on Savita (the Sun) as Brahman, the wise ones strive for Him,
With a pleasing speech before me.

Om Devi Gayatri, may you be released from the curse of Brahma. (Recite three times)

2. Vishvamitra Curse Release –
For this Sri Vishvamitra Curse Release Mantra, Vishvamitra is the rishi who creates anew, Vagdevya (Vaghevi) is the meter, the Gayatri Shakti pleased by Vishvamitra, Savita is the deity, for release from Vishvamitra's curse the application is japa.

Mantra –
The elements in the limbs, known by fire, the thoughts, the triply-born one from whom the gods created the universe at the outset, that auspicious one granting all desires, the source of the Vedas, I take refuge.

Om Devi Gayatri, may you be released from the curse of Vishvamitra. (Recite three times)

3. Vashishtha Curse Release –
For this Sri Vashishtha Curse Release Mantra, Vashishtha is the rishi, Vishvodbhava (world-born) is the meter, the Gayatri Shakti pleased by Vashishtha, Savita is the deity, for release from Vashishtha's curse the application is japa.

Mantra –
The elements in the limbs, known by fire, the thoughts that contemplate the weapons of Vishnu, which all beings know as the Supreme and Eternal, Gayatri who is praised as the excellent abode.

Om Devi Gayatri, may you be released from the curse of Vashishtha. (Recite three times)

Prayer –
I am the Sun, I am light, I am solar light, I am Shiva,
I am Self-light, I am white, I am white light, I am Om.

O Lord, in the divine, accomplished Sarasvati, who is immortal and ageless,
Born of Brahman, salutations to You.
