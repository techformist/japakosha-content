angirasa uvaaca |
    anantaa avataaraashca gaNeshasya maahaatmanaH |
    na shakyatei kathaaam vaktuN maayaa varShashatirapi || 1 ||

saNkSheiNa pravakShyaami mukhyaanaam mukhyataa gataan |
    avataaraamshca tasyaaShTau viK hyaataaN brahmadhaarakaan || 2 ||

vakratuNDaavatarashca deihi naa brahmadhaarakaH |
    matsuraasurahantaa sa siNhavahanagaH shmRutaH || 3 ||

aiekadataaavataro vai deihi naa brahmadhaarakaH |
    madaa surasya hantaa sa aakhuvahanagaH shmuRutaH || 4 ||

mahodara iti Kh yaato jnaanabrahmaprakaashakaH |
    mohaasurasya shatruvai aakhuvahanagaH shmuRutaH || 5 ||

gajaananaH sa vijeyaH saaNKhyebhyaH siddhidaayakaH |
    lobhaasuraprahartaa ca muuShakagaH prakIrtitaH || 6 ||

lambodaraavataro vai kraDhaasuranibarhaNaH |
    aakhugaH shaktibrahmaa san tasya dhaaraka ucyatei || 7 ||

vikaTo naama viK hyaataH kaamaasurapradaahakaH |
    mayuuravahanashcaayam sauraatmadharaH smRutaH || 8 ||

vighNaraajaavatarashca sheShavahana ucyatei |
    mamaasuraprahaNtaa sa vishNubrahmeti vaacakam || 9 ||

dhuumravarNaavatarashcaabhimaanasuranaashakaH |
    aakhuvahanataa praaapta shivaatmakaH ucyatei || 10 ||

aite'ShTau tei mayaa prokti gaNeshaa mshau vinaayakaaH |
    eiShaabhaj anamaatreiNa svashva-brahmapradhaarakaaH || 11 ||

shvaanandava sakaarii sa gaNeshaaH prakathyatei |
    shvaanandei yogibhirdRiShTo brahmaNi naatra samshayaH || 12 ||

tasyaavata raaru paashca aShTau vighnaharaNaah shmRutaaH |
    shvaanandabhajaneiva leelaastatra bhavanti hi || 13 ||

maayaa tatra svayam liinaa bhavi shyati suputraka |
    saN yogeim mabhaava shca samaadhiH praap yatei janaiH || 14 ||

ayogei gaNaraajasya bhajanei naiva siddhyatei |
    maayaabhedamayam brahma nirvRuti praap yatei paraa || 15 ||

yogaatmaka gaNeshaaNo brahmaNaspativaacakam |
    tatra shaantiH samaak hyaataa yogaruupaa janaiH kritaa || 16 ||

naanaa shaantipramodashca sthaanei sthaanei prakathyatei |
    shaantiinNaam shaantiiru paa saa yogashaantiH prakIrtitaa || 17 ||

yogasya yogataa dRiShTaa sarvabrahma suputraka |
    na yogaatparamam brahma brahmabhuutena labhyatei || 18 ||

eitadeiva paraM guhyam kathitam vatsa te'liikham |
    bhaja tvaM sarvabhaavena gaNeshaM brahmanaayakam || 19 ||

putrapautraadipradam stotramidaM shokavinashanam |
    dhanadhanyasamRi dhy aadipradam bhaavi na samshayaH || 20 ||

dharmaarthakaamamokShaaNaam saadhanam brahmad aayakam |
    bhaktidRiidakaraM caiva bhavi shyati na samshayaH || 21 ||
