||amatyaputravadhah ||
tataste rakshasendrena chodita mantrinah sutah |
niryayurbhavanat tasmata sapta saprarchivarhasah || 1 ||

mabalaparivara dhanushmanto mahabalah |
kritastrastravidaan shreshthah parasparajaishinah || 2 ||

hemajalaparikshitair dhajavadbhih patakibhih |
toyadasvanani ghoshair vajiyuktai mharathaih || 3 ||

taptakanchanacitrani capanyamitavikramah |
vispharayah samhrishtatitvanta ivambudhah || 4 ||

jananyastu tatesham viddva kinkaran hathan |
babhuvah shokasabhamhrah sabandhassuhrujanah || 5 ||

te parasparasamghrashat tapakanchanabhushanah |
abhipeturhanumamt toranasthamavasthitam || 6 ||

srajanto banavrishim te rathagarjitanihsvanah |
vishrimanta ivambhodha vichururnairitamabdudhah || 7 ||

avakirnastatabhirhanumane sharavrishibhih |
abhavatsamavatakah shailaradiva vrishibhih || 8 ||

sa sharannmoghayamasha teshamashucharah kapiah |
rathavegam cha viranam vicaran vimalambhare || 9 ||

sa taih kridandhanushmibhir vyomni virah prakashate |
dhanushmibhir yatha megaih marutih prabhumambhare || 10 ||

sa krtvaninadam ghoram trasayansta mahachamum |
chakara hanumanvegam teshu rakshasu viryavan || 11 ||

taleanabhyahanatkanchitpadbhya kamshichiparantapah |
[padaih]
mushtinabhyahanatkanchimnakhaih kamshichidyadarayat || 12 ||

pramamathourasa kamshichidurubhyamaparan kapiah |
kechit tasya ninadena tatraiva patita bhavi || 13 ||

tatasteshavasanneshu bhau nipatiteshu cha |
tatsainyamagatasarvam disho dasha bhayarditam || 14 ||

vinedurvisvaram naga nipeturbhavi vajinah |
bhaganidadhvajachatribhuh kiranarthaih || 15 ||

sravata rudhirena sravantyah darshitah pathi |
vividhaishcha svarair lanka nanada vikritam tada || 16 ||

sa tanpravrddhanihanihthy rakshasa-
-namahabalashandaparakramah kapiah |
yuyutsuranyaih punareva rakshasai-
-stadeva viroabhijagama toranam || 17 ||
