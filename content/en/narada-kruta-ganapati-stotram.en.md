narada uvaca |
bho ganeshas sureshtha lambodara paratpara |
heramba mangalarambha gajavaktra trilochana || 1 ||

muktida shubhada shrida shridharasmarane rata |
paramananda parama parvatinandana svayam || 2 ||

sarvatra pujya sarvesha jagatpujya mahamate |
jagadguro jagannatha jagadesha namo'stu te || 3 ||

yatpuja sarvapurato yah stutah sarvayogibhih |
yah pujitah surendraish ca munindraistam namamyaham || 4 ||

paramaradhanaiva krishnasya paramatmanah |
punyakena vratena yam prapa parvati sati || 5 ||

tam namami sureshtham sarvareshtam garishthaka |
jnanishreshtam varishtham ca tam namami ganeshvaram || 6 ||

ityevamuktvaa devarshistatraivaa'ntardadhe vibhuh |
naradah prayayau sheghram-ishvaraabhya'ntaram mudaa || 7 ||

idam lambodarastotram naarenaa krutam puraa |
pujaakaale pataen-nityam jayam tasya pade pade || 8 ||

sankalpitam patedyo hi varshamekam susanyatah |
vishishtaputram labhate param krishnaparayanam || 9 ||

yasvinam ca vidvaansam dhaninam cirajivinam |
vighanasho bhavettasya mahaishvaryam yasho'malam |
ihaiva ca sukham bhaktyaa ante yaati hareh padam || 10 ||
