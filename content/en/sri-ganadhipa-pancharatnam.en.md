saraagilokaduradhabam viraagilokapoojitam |


suraasuraih namaskritam jaraapamrityunaashakam || 1 ||


    giraa gurum shriyaa hariim jayanti yatpadaarchakaa


namaami tam gaanaadhipam kripaapayoh payonidhim || 1 ||





giriindrajaamukhaambujapramodaanaabhaaskaram |


kariindravaktramaataaghasanghavaaranodyatam || 2 ||


    sariirspeesha-baddhakukshimaashrayaami santatam


shariirakaantinirjitaabjabandubaalasantatim || 2 ||





shukaadimaunivanditam gakaaravaacyamaksharam |


prakaamamiishtadaayinam sakaanamanrapanktaye || 3 ||


    cakaasatam caturbhuaiirkaasi-padmapoojitam


prakaashitaamatmatattvaam namaamyaham gaanaadhipam || 3 ||





araadhipatvadaayakam saraadilokanaayakam |


jvaraadirogavarakanairaakrutaasuravajam || 4 ||


    karaaambujollasatsriniim kaarashoonyamaanasaih


hridaa sadaa vibhaavitam mudaa namaami vighnapam || 4 ||





shramaapanodanakshamam samaahitaantaraatmanaam |


sumaadhiih sadaarchitam kshaamaanidhim gaanaadhipam || 5 ||


    raamadhavaadipoojitam yaamaantakaatmasambhavam


shamaadishadgunapradam namaamyaham vibhutayei || 5 ||





gaanaadhipasya panchakam nrunaamabhiishthadaayakam |


pranaamapurvakam janaah pathanti ye mudaa yutaah || 6 ||


    bhavanti te vedaanah purah prageetavaibhaavaa javaata


hiraayusho'dhika shriiyh susuunavo na samshayah || 6 ||
