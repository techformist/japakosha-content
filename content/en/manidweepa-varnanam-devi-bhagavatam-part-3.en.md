vyAsa uvAcha |
    tatadeva sadanamadhyabhAge virA-jate |
    saha- rastaM bhassAm-chatvArAsteShu maM- dapAH || 1||

shRUgArAma- dapashcha eka mukti- dap- eva cha |
    jnAna- dapasajYastu tRitIyaH parikI- titaH || 2||

eka- ntAma- dapashchaiva chaturthaH parikI- titaH |
    nAnAvitAnasau- yuktA nAnAdhUpaistudhUpitAH || 3||

koTisUryasamAH kAntyA bhAj- ante maM- dapAH shubhAH |
    tanma- MpANAm paritaH kAshmIra- vanikA smRitA || 4||

mallikAku- ndavanikA yatra puSh- kalAH sthitAH |
    asaMkhyAtAmRugamadaiH pUritAstatasravaA nRupa || 5||

mahA- padmATavI tadvadratna- sopAnanirmitA |
    sudhA- rasena sampUrNA gu~gumattamadhuvratA || 6||

haM sakArNDavAkIrNA gandhapUritadikaTTA |
    vanikAnAM sugandhaistumaNi- dIpAMasu vAsitaM || 7||

shRUgArAma- debeAvo gAyanti vividhaiH svaraiH |
    sabhAsadoA devaAvarA madhyae shrI-jagadam- bikA || 8||

mukti- dapamadhyae tu mocayatyaniSham ShivA |
    jnAnopadeShaM kurute tRitIyae nRupa- maM- dape || 9||

chaturthAma- dapashchaiva jagadar- kAvicintanamaM |
    maNtRiNI sahithA nityaM karoti jagadam- bikA || 10||

chintAmaNigRuhe rAjana shaktitatvAtmaikaparaH |
    sopAnaidashabhira yuktaH manchako'pyadhIrAjate || 11||

brahmA viShNuH cha rudraH cha I-shvaraH cha sadAshivaH |
    ete maM- khurAH proktAH phalakastu sadAshivaH || 12||

tasyopari mahAdevo bhavaneIro virAjate |
    yA devI nija- lIlAr- thaM dvidhA bhUtA babhUva ha || 13||

sRiShTAdau tu sa evAyaM tadardhAgo maheshvaraH |
    kandarpadarpa- nA-UdyakoTikandarpasu- ndaraH || 14||

pachavaktatrInetraH cha maNibhUShaNabhUShitaH |
    hariNIbhitiparashU- navaraM cha nijabAhubhiH || 15||

dadhANaH ShoShA- bdasho'sau devaH sarveIvaraH mahAnaH |
    koTisUrya- pratIkAshachandra- koTisushItalaH || 16||

shuddha- SphaTikasaM kAHAH trinetraH shItaladyutiH |
    vAmAMke saM niShNAsya devI shrIbhavaneI- varI || 17||

navaratnagaNAkIrNA- kA nchIdAmavirAjitA |
    taptakA- nanasaM naddavaidUryA- gadabhUShaN_A || 18||

ka~cchShicakra- tAkaM- viTanka-vadanaM- bujA |
    lAlATIkantivibhavavijitA- rdhasudhAkArA || 19||

bimbAkantitiraskAriradachchadavirAjitA |
    lasatkuku- makastU- rItilakodbhAsitanAnA || 20||

divyacUdAma- nispharacacancandrakAsUryakA |
    udyatkavi- samasvachchhanAsA- bharaNAbhAsurA || 21||

chintAkala- mbitasvachchhamukat-A guccavirAjitA |
    pATIr- aparNkapUrpaku- mmAlAMku- mastanI || 22||

vichitrA- vividhAkalapA kam- basaM kAshandharA |
    dA- dImIphalAbhAda- ntapa- ktivirAjitA || 23||

anarghyaratnaghA- tamuMtAMchitaM astakA |
    mattA- limAlAvilasadAlakA- ZyamukhAM- bujA || 24||

kalAbkA- shyAnirmuktasharchchandra- nibhAnA |
    jAhnavIsalilAvartashobhinAbhibhUShitA || 25||

mANikyashakalAbaddhamudrikA- guLibhUShitA |
    puN- drIkadalA- kAranayanatrayasu- ndarI || 26||

kalpItAchcha- mahArAgapadamarAgojjvalaprabhA |
    ratnakikiNikAyukaratnakankaNashobhitA || 27||

maNimuktAsarApAralasatpa- dakasamatatiH |
    ratnAguLipravitataprabhAjAlalasatkaraH || 28||

ka~cukUgiMfitApAranAnA- ratnatatidyuatiH |
    mallikAmodidhamillamallikA- lisarAvRRutA || 29||

suvRutanibidautuMgakuchabhArAlasA ShivA |
    varapAshAMkUshAbhiitalasadabAhuchatuShTayA || 30||

sarvashRUgArAvaiDhyA su- kumArAM- gavallarI |
    sau- daryAsarvashvA nRuvyAjakaruNAmayI || 31||

nijasallApAmadhuryavini- bhaRshichichChapI |
    koTikoTiravIndUnAM kAntiM yA bibhratI parA || 32||

nAnAsakhIbidAdAsibatha devAM- gadibhiH |
    sarvAbhirdaI- tibhastu samAntAtpariveShTitA || 33||

ichChAshiktyA jnAnAshiktyA kriyAshaktyA samanvitA |
    lajjA tuShTitathA puShTiH kIrtiH kAntiH kShamA dayA || 34||

buddhirmedhA smRitirlakShmImUratimato'nganAH smRitAH |
    jayA cha vijayA chaivApyajitA chaparAjitA || 35||

nityA vilAsinI doghrI tvaghorA maNgala navA |
    pIThAshaktaya eTAstu sevaMte yAM parA- mbikAm || 36||

yasyAstu pArShvabhAge stau nidhItau shaM kha- padma- kau |
    navaratnavahA nadyastathA vai kA- canasravAH || 37||

saptadhAtuvahA nadyo nidhibhyAstu vinirgatAH |
    sudhAsi- mdhavAgA- minyAstAH sarvA nRupa- sattama || 38||

sA devI bhavaneI- tadvAmAMke virAjate |
    sarveIshatvaM maheshvasya tasaM gAdeva nAnyathA || 39||

chintAmaNigRuhasya pramANaM ShRinu bhUmi- pataH |
    saha- trayojanAyAmaM mahAnstatatprachakShate || 40||

tadauttare mahAshAlAH pUrvAsmAddviguNAsaH smRitAH |
    antarikShagataM tvevanirAdhAmaM virAjate || 41||

saMkoShacha vikAshacha jAyate'sya nirantaram |
    paTavatkAryavasataH praLaye sa- rjane tathA || 42||

shAlAnAM chaiva sarveShAM sarvakAntiparAvadhi |
    chintAmaNigRupaM proktaM yatra devI mahomayI || 43||

ye ye upAsakAH saMti pratibrahmANDavartinAH |
    deveShu nAgalokeShu manuSheShvitare cha || 44||

shrIdevyAste cha sarve'pya vraM yatrAiva bhUmipataH |
    devIkShetre ye tyajanti prANAMdevyarchane ratAH || 45||

te sarve yAnti tatraiva yatra devI mahotsavA |
    ghatIkuliyA dugdHakuliyA dadhikuliyA madhusravAH || 46||

syandanti saritAH sarvAstathAmRitavahA paraH |
    drAkShArasavahA kAshcijjaMbhUrasavahA paraH || 47||

AmrekShurasavAhinyo nadAyAstu sahasraH |
    manorathaphalA vRikShAH vApyaH kUpAstathaiva cha || 48||

yathAshTApAnaphaladA na nUnaM kiM chidasti hi |
    na rogapalitaM vApi jarA vApi kadAchana || 49||

nA chintA na cha mA tsaryA kAmakrodhAdikaM tathA |
    sarve yuvANaHasastrIkAH sahasrAdityavarchasaH || 50||

bhajanti satataM devIma tatra shrIbhavaneI- varIm |
    kechitasalolAtAnAM kechitsA- mIpyatA gatAH || 51||

sarUpatAM gatAH kechitsARshTitAM cha pare gatAH |
    yA yAstu devatAstatra pratibrahmANDavartinAM || 52||

sashTayAH sthitAstAH tu sevaMte jagadIshvarIm |
    saptakoTimahAmantRAnmUrtimanto upAsate || 53||

mahAvidyAcha sakalAH sAmyAvasthAtmikAm ShivAm |
    kAraNabrahmarUpAm tAm mAyAshabalavigrahAm || 54||

itihaM rAjana mayA proktaM maNidIpamaM mahattaram |
    na sUryachandrAno na vidyutkoTayo'gnistathaiva cha || 55||

etasya bhAsA koTyaMshakoTyaMshena'pi te samAH |
    kvachidvidrumasakaM kvachinmarakatachchavi || 56||

vidyudbhAnusamachChAya madhyasUryasamaM kvachitaM |
    vidyukoTimahAdhArA sArAkantitataM kvachitaM || 57||

kvachitasindUranIlAmAqikyasAdRishachchavi |
    hIrAsAraMmahA-garbhadhagaddhagitadikaTTamaM || 58||

kAntyA dAvAnalasamaM taptakA- nasaM nibham |
    kvachichandro- palodgAraM sUryodgAraM cha kutrachitaM || 59||

ratnashRUgisamAyuktaM ratnaprAkAragopuramaM |
    ratnapatrai ratnaphalaiH vRikShaiHcha parimaM- ditamaH || 60||

nRutyamayUrasaGghaiH kapoTa- iraNojjvalamaM |
    kokilA-kAkAlIlApaiH shukalApaiH cha shobhitamaM || 61||

suramyara maNIyAmbulakShAvadhisarovRRutamaM |
    tanmadhyabhAgavilasadavichad ratnapankajaiH || 62||

sugandhibhiH samantA tu vAsitaM shatayoajanamaM |
    madamAra tusambhinachaladrumasAmakulamaM || 63||

chintAmaNisamU hAnAM jyotiShA vitAmbaramaM |
    ratnaprabhAbhirahito dhagaddhagitadikaTTamaM || 64||

vRukShavrAtamahAgandha-vAtA-vAtasupUritamaM |
    dhUpadhUpAyitaM rAjnamaNidIpAyatojjvalamaM || 65||

maNijAlakasachchidratataralodarAkAntibhiH |
    diMgomajanaKamaM chaitaddarpaNodarasaMyauatamaM || 66||

aishvaryasya samagrasya shRUgArAsyAkhilasya cha |
    sarvajAtAyA sarvAyAstejashchAkhilasya cha || 67||

parAkramasya sarvasya sarvottamaguNasya cha |
    sakalAyA dayAyAsha cha samAptiriha bhUpate || 68||

rAjA AnandamArabhyabrahmalokAnta-bhUmiShu |
    AnandA ye sthitAH sarvAste'traivantaraM bhavantihai || 69||

iti te varNitaM rAjanna- madIpamamahattaram |
    mahAdevyAparaM sthAnaM sarvalokauttamottamamaM || 70||

etasya smaraNAtsadayaH sarvaM pApamavinashyati |
    praNotakramaM-sandhau tu smRutvA tatraiva gacChati || 71||

adhyAya paM chakam tveitaM paThIhaM nitya samAhitaH |
    bhUtapreshApi- chAdibhAdA tatra bhavennaM hi || 72||

navInagRahanirmaNa vAstuyAge tathaiva cha |
    paThitavyaM prayatena kalyANaM tena jAyate || 73||
