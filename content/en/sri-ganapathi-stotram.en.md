jetum yastripuram harena hariinaa vyaajaa balim badhnataa |
stratum vaaribhavodbhavena bhuvanam sheNa dhartum dharam || 1 ||
    paarvathyaa mahishaasurapramathanei siddhaadhipaih siddhayei
dhyaatah panchaarena vishvajitayei paayaata sa naagaanahah || 1 ||

vighnadvaantanivaaraNaikataraNirvighNaaTaviihyavvaaT |
vighnavyaalakulaabhimaanagarudo vighaibhapanchaananaah || 2 ||
    vighnautuNgagiriprabhedanapavirvighNaambudheirvaaDavo
vighNaaghauDhaghanapraChaNDapavano vighNaeshvarah paatu nahah || 2 ||

kharvam stuu latanum gajeindra-vadanam lambodaram sundaram |
prasyandamadagandalubdhamadhupavyaalolagandasthalam || 3 ||
    daNTaaghaaTavidaaritaarirudhiraihi siindhuura shobhakara
vandei shailasutaasutam ganapatim siddhipradam kaamadam || 3 ||

gajaanaanaaya mahasei pratyuu hatimirachchidei |
    aapaarakaruNaa pooratarangitadrishei namah || 4 ||

agajaanaanapadmaarkam gajaanaanamahar nisham |
    aneikadam tam bhaktaanaamaiekantamupaasmahe || 5 ||

shvetaangam shvetavastram sitakusumaganaih pujitam shvetagandaih |
kshiiraabdhou ratnadiipaih suranaratiilam ratnasinhaasanastham || 6 ||
    dorbhih paashaankuashaabjaabhayavaramanasaam chandramaulim trinetram
dhyaayeichaantyarthamiisham ganapatimamalam shriisametam prasannam || 6 ||

aavaahayei tam ganaraajadevam raktotpaalaabhaamasheshavand yam |
    vighNaantakam vighnaharam ganeishaam bhajaami raudram sahitam ca siddhyaa || 7 ||

yam brahma vedaantavidao vadaati param pradhaanam puruSham tathaa'nyai |
    vishvaudgateH kaaraNamiiishvaraa vaa tasmai namo vighnavinaashanaaya || 8 ||

vighNaesha viiryaNi vicitrakaaNi vandeejanairmaagadhakaih shmrutaNi |
    shrutvaa samuttishTha gajaanana tvaam braahmei jagammangalakam kuruShava || 9 ||

ganesha heramba gajaanaaneti mahodara svanubhavaprakaashin |
    variishTha siddhiprii buddhinaatha vadata aivaam tyajata prabhiitiih || 10 ||

aneikavighNaantaka vakratuNDa svasaMjnavaasiiMshca caturbhuEti |
    kaviiisha devaantakanaashaikaarin vadata aivaam tyajata prabhiitiih || 11 ||

anantacidruapamayam ganeishaam hyabhedabhed aadivihiinamaadyam |
    hridi prakaashasya dharam svaadhiiStham tameikantam sharaNam vraajaamah || 12 ||

vishvaadibhuutam hridi yoginaa vai pratyakSharupeNa vibhaantameikam |
    sadaa niraalambasamaadhigamyaam tameikantam sharaNam vraajaamah || 13 ||

yadlii viiryeNa samarthabhuutaa mayaa tayaa samarachitam ca vishvam |
    naagaatmakam hyaatmatayaa pratiitam tameikantam sharaNam vraajaamah || 14 ||

sarvaantarei samsthitameikamuuDham yadaaJNayaa sarvamidam vibhaati |
    anantaruPam hridi bodakam vai tameikantam sharaNam vraajaamah || 15 ||

yam yogino yogabalena saadhyam kurvanti tam kah stavanaanauti |
    ataH praNAmenasusiddhido'stu tameikantam sharaNam vraajaamah || 16 ||

devaimauliimandaraamakaraNTaaruuNaah |
    vighNaan harantu herambacharaNaambujareiNavah || 17 ||

aiekantam mahaakaayaam lambodara gajaananaam |
    vighNaasha karam devam herambam praNAmAmyaham || 18 ||

yadakShara pada bhraShTam maatraahiinam ca yadbhavet |
    tatsarvam kshamyataam deva prasiida paramaiishvara || 19 ||
