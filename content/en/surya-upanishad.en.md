om bhadram karnebhih shrnuyama devah | bhadram pashyemakshabhiryajatrah | sthirairangai stuvagmasa stanubhih | vyashema devahitam yadayuh | svasti na indro viddhashravah | svasti nah pusha vishvedevah | svasti nastarkshyo arishthanemih | svasti no brihaspatirdadhatu ||
om shantih shantih shantih ||

om atha suryatharvangirasam vyakhyasyamah | brahma rishih | gayatri chandah | adityo devata | hamso vahamagranarayana yuktam bijam | hrllekha shakti | vyadadi sargasamyuktam kilakam | chaturvidha purushartha siddhyarthe viniyogah |

shatsvararudhina bijena shadangam raktambujasamsthitam saptashvarathinam hiranyavarnam chaturbhujam padmadvyabhayavaradahastam kalachakrapranetaram shrisuryanarayanam ya evam veda sa vai brahmanah ||

om bhurbhuvah suh | tatsaviturvarenyam bhargo devasya dhimahi | dhiyo yo nah prachodayat |
surya atma jagatastasthushcha |
suryadvai khvalimani bhutani jayante |
suryadyajnah parjanyo amnatma |

namaste aditya | tvameva pratyaksham karma kartasi | tvameva pratyaksham brahmasi | tvameva pratyaksham vishnurasi | tvameva pratyaksham rudro asi | tvameva pratyakshamrigasi | tvameva pratyaksham yajurasi | tvameva pratyaksham samasi | tvameva pratyaksham atharvasi | tvameva sarvam chandosi |
adityadvayurjayate | adityadbhumirjayate | adityadapo jayante | adityadyotirjayate | adityadvyoma disho jayante | adityaddava jayante | adityaddveda jayante |
adityo va esha etanmandalam tapati | asavasuryo brahma | adityo antahakarana manobuddhi chittamkarah | adityo vai vyana samanodanapranah |
adityo vai shrotra tvak chakshurasanghranah | adityo vai vakpanipadapayuupasthah | adityo vai shabdasparsharuparasagandhah |adityo vacanadanagamanavisarganandah |anandamayo vijanamayo vijananaghana adityah |namo mitraya bhanave mrtyorma pahi | bhajishnave vishhahetave namah |

suryadbhavanti bhutani surena palitani tu |surye layam prapnuvanti yah suryah so aham eva cha |
chakshurno devah savita chakshurna uta paravatah | chakshurdhata dadhatu nah |

adityayavidmahe sahasrakirnayadhima hi | tannah suryah prachodayat |

sativa pashchat tata savita purastata savitottarata tata savitadharata tata | savita nah savatu sarvatah atigam | savita no rasatam dirghamayuh |

omityekaksharam brahma | ghriritiridve akshare | surya ityaksharadvayam | aditya iti trinyaksharani | etasyaiva suryasyashtaksharo manuh |

yah sadaharahahar japati sa vai brahmanoh bhavati sa vai brahmanoh bhavati |suryabhimukho japtva mahavyadhi bhayata pramucyate |alakshmirnashyati |abhakshya bhakshanata putobhavati |agamyagamanata putobhavati |patitasambhashanata putobhavati |asat sambhashanata putobhavati |asat sambhashanataputobhavati |

madhyahne suryabhimukah patheh |sadyotpannapanchamahapatakaat pramucyate |saisha saitrividya na kinchidapi na kasmaicita prashamsayeta |ya etam mahabhagah pratah pathati sa bhagyavan jayate | pashum vindati | vedartham labhate | trikalameit japtva kratu shata phalamavapnoti | hastadityai japati sa mahamrityum tarati sa mahamrityum tarati ya evam veda |ityupanishat ||
