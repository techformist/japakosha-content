namaste deva-deveShe yogeShe-prANavallabhe |
    siddhIde varade mAtAH svayambhUliMgaveShTit-e || 1||

prasupta-bhujAgAkAre sarvadA kAraNapriye |
    kAmakalAnvitA devI mamAbhiShTaM kuruShva cha || 2||

asAre ghorasaMsAre bhavarogAt kuLesHvarI |
    sarvadA rakSha mAM devI janmasaMsArasAgarAt || 3||

iti kundalini stotraM dhyAtvA yaH praPathate sudhIH |
    mucyate sarva pApebhyo bhavasaMsArarUpake || 4||
