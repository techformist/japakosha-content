smaraami devadevesham vakratundam mahaabalam |
shadaksharam kripaasindhum namaami runa mukataye || 1 ||

ekaaksharam hyekadamantam ekam brahma sanaatanam |
ekamevaadvitiiyam ca namaami runa mukataye || 2 ||

mahaaganapatim devam mahaasattvam mahaabalam |
mahaavighnaharam shambhoh namaami runa mukataye || 3 ||

krishnaamabaram krishnavarnam krishnagandhanulepanam |
krishnas aropavitam ca namaami runa mukataye || 4 ||

raktaamabaram rakataarnam raktagandhanulepanam |
rakta pushp apriyam devam namaami runa mukataye || 5 ||

peetaamabaram peetavarnam peetagandhanulepanam |
peetapushp apriyam devam namaami runa mukataye || 6 ||

dhuumraamabaram dhuumraavarnam dhuumragandhanulepanam |
homadhuuma apriyam devam namaami runa mukataye || 7 ||

phaalanetram phaalachandram paashaankushadharam vibhum |
caamaaraalankritam devam namaami runa mukataye || 8 ||

idam tvrunaharam stotram sandhyaayaam yah pataen-arah |
shanmaasaabhya'ntarenaiva runa mukto bhavishyati || 9 ||
