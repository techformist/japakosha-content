OM saha nAvavatu |
    saha nau bhunaktu
|| atha prathamaH khaNdaH |
    athAshvAlAyano bhagavantaM parameShThinamupasametyovAcha |
    adhIhi bhagavanbrahmavidyA varishThAm sadA sadbhibh sevyamAnAm nIgUdhAm |
    yathAchirAt sarvapApaM vyapohya parAtparama puruShaM yAti vidvAn || 1||

tasmai sa havAcha pitAmahaH cha shraddhA-bhakti-dhyAna-yogAdavaihi || 2||

na karmaNA prajayA dhanena tyAgAkaikAmRtatvAManashaH |
    parEnAkaM nihitaM guhAyAM vibhrAjasatyadayo vishyanti || 3||

vedAnta-vijnAna-suniccitArthAH saNnyAsa-yogAdayataH shuddha-sattvAH |
    te brahmalokeShu parAntakAle parAmRutAH parimucyanti sarve || 4||

vivikta-deshe cha sukhasanasthaH shuchiH samagrI-shirash-sharIraH |
    antyAshramasthaH sakalendriyANi nirdhya bhaktyA svaguruM praNamyA || 5||

hRtpuNDarIkaM virajaM vishuddhaM vichintya madhye vishadaM vishokam |
    achintyamavyaktamAnantarUpaM shivaM prAshAntamAmRutaM brahma-yonim || 6||

tamAdi-madhyAnta-vihInamekaM vibhuM chidAnandaM-arUpamadbhutam |
    umAsahAyaM parameShvaraM prabhuM trilochanaM nIla-kaNTaM prAshAntam |
    dhyAtvA munirgacchati bhUtayoniM samasta-sAkShiM tamasaH parastAt || 7||

sa brahmA sa shivaH sendraH so'kSharaH paraH svarAt |
    sa eva viShNuH sa prANaH sa kAlo'gniH sa chandramAaH || 8||

sa eva sarvaM yadbhUtam yachcha bhavyaH sanAtanam |
    jnAtvA taM mRtyumatyeti nAnyaH panthA vimuktaye || 9||

sarvabhUtasthamAtmAnam sarvabhUtAni chAtmani |
    sampashyanbrahma paramaM yAti nAnAnyena hetunA || 10||

AtmAnA-araNiM kRtvA praNavam chottarAraNim |
    jnAna-nirmathanAbhyAsAt pApaM dahati paNDitaH || 11||

sa eva mAyA-parimohitAtmA sharIramAstHAn karoti sarvam |
    strIyam-apAnAdi-vichitra-bhogaiH sa eva jAgrati-paritRuptimeti || 12||

svapne sa jIvaH sukhaduHkha-bhoktA svamAyayA kalpita-jIvaloke |
    suShuptikAle sakale vilInaH tamobhUtaH sukhArUpameti || 13||

punaH cha janmAntara-karma-yogAt sa eva jIvaH swapiti prabuddhaH |
    puratraye krIDati yashcha jIvastastu jAtaM sakalavicitraM |
    AdhArAMAnandaM-akhaNDa-bodhaM yasmin-layAm yAti puratrayaM cha || 14||

etasmAj-jAyate prANo manaH sarvendriyANi cha |
    khaM vAyurjyotirApaH pRithivI vishvasya dhAriNee || 15||

yatparaM brahma sarvAtmA vishvasyAayatanaM mahat |
    sUkshmAtsUkShyataraM nityaMtattvaMeva tvameva tataH || 16||

jAgratasvapna-suShuptyAdi-prapanchaM yatprakAshate |
    tadbrahmAhamiti jnAtvA sarvabandhaiH pramucyate || 17||

tRiShu dhAmasu yat-bhogyam bhoktA bhogashcha yadbhavet |
    tebhyaH vilakShaNAH sAkShee chitmAtrOahaM sadAshivaH || 18||

mayeva sakalaM jAtaM mayi sarvaM pratiShThitam |
    mayi sarvaM layaM yAti tadbrahmAdvayamasmyaham || 19||

|| atha dvitIyaH khaNdaH |
    aNoraNIyAnahameva tadvana mahAnaham vishvamavichitram |
    purAtano'haM puruSho'haM ISho hiraNamayo'haM shivarUpamasmi || 20||

apAnipAdayahamachintya-shaktiH pashyAmi chakShuH sa shRuNomakarNaH |
    ahaM vijAnAmi vivikta-RUpo nAchAsti vettA mama chitsadAham || 21||

vedaiRanekaiHahameva vedyo vedAntakRutda-vida-eva chAham || 22||

na puNyapApe mama nAshaH na janma dehendriya-buddhirasti |
    na bhUmirApo na chavahnirasti nAchAnilo me'asti nAchAmbaraM cha || 23||

evaM viditvA paramAtma-RUpaM guhAshayaM niShkalamadvitIyam |
    samasta-sAkShiM sadasadvihInaM prayAti shuddhaM paramAtma-RUpam || 24||

yaH shatadRudriyamadhIte so'gnipUto bhavati sa vAyupUto bhavati sa AtmapUto bhavati sa surApAnAtpUto bhavati sa brahmahatyAyAH pUto bhavati sa suvarNasteyAtpUto bhavati sa kRutyAkRutyAtpUto bhavati tasmAdavimuktamaAshRito bhavatyatyAshramees varadA sa sakRidvA japeta || 25||

anena jnAnAMApnoti saMsArArNavanAshanam |
    tasmAdevaM viditvainaM kaivalyaM padamashnute kaivalyaM padamashnuta iti || 26||

OM saha nAvavatu |
    saha nau bhunaktu
