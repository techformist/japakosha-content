dritrivam devadevasya rupam bhanormahatmanah |
vismayotphullanayanastustavaste divakaram || 1 ||

kritanjaliputo bhutva brahma stotum prachakrame |
pranamya shirasa bhanumidam vachanamabravit || 2 ||

brahmuvacha |
namaste devadevesha sahasrakiranojjvala |
lokadipa namastvu namaste konavallabha || 3 ||

bhaskaraya namo nityam khasholkaya namo namah |
vishnave kalachakraya somayamitategase || 4 ||

namaste panchakalaya indraya vasuretase |
khagaya lokanathaya eka chakrarathaya cha || 5 ||

jagadhitaya devaya shivayamitategase |
tamoghanaya surupaya tejasam nidhaye namah || 6 ||

arthaya kamarupaya dharmayamitategase |
mokshaya moksharupaya suryaya cha namo namah || 7 ||

krodhalobhavihinaya lokanam sthitihetave |
shubhaya shubharupaya shubhadaya shubhatmane || 8 ||

shantaya shantarupaya shantayasmai vai namah |
namaste brahmarupaya brahmanaya namo namah || 9 ||

brahmadevaya brahmarupaya brahmane paramatmane |
brahmane cha prasadam vai kura deva jagatpate || 10 ||

evam stutva ravin brahma shraddhaya paraya vibho |
tushnimasinmahabhaga prahrishtenantarmanah || 11 ||

brahmanantaram rudrah stotram chakre vibhavasoh |
tripurarirmahatejah pranamya shirasa ravim || 12 ||

mahadev uvacha |
jaya bhava jayajeya jaya hansa divakara |
jaya shambho mahabaho khaga gocara bhuadhara || 13 ||

jaya lokapradevena jaya bhano jagatpate |
jaya kala jayamanta samvatsara shubhanana || 14 ||

jaya devavaditeh putra kashyapananda vardhana |
tamoghana jaya saptesh jaya saptashvavahana || 15 ||

grahesh jaya kantish jaya kalisha shankara |
arthakamesh dharmesh jayamokshesh sharmada || 16 ||

jaya vedangarupaya graharupaya vai gatah |
satyaya satyarupaya surupaya shubhaya cha || 17 ||

krodhalobhavinashaya kamanashaya vai jaya |
kalmashapakshirupaya yatirupaya shambhave || 18 ||

vishvaya vishvarupaya vishvakarmaya vai jaya |
jayomkara vashatkara svahakara svadhaya cha || 19 ||

jayashvamedharupaya chagniruparyamaya cha |
samsararnavapitaya mokshadwarapradaya cha || 20 ||

samsararnavamagnasya mama deva jagatpate |
hastavalambano deva bhava tvam gopateadbhuta || 21 ||

ishopyevaminehangam stutva bhanum prayatnatah |
viraraja maharaja pranamya shirasa ravim || 22 ||

atha vishnurmahatejah kritanjaliputo ravim |
uvacha rashardhula bhaktya shraddhasamanvitah || 23 ||

vishnuruvacha |
namami devadevasham bhutabhavanamavyayam |
divakaram ravim bhanum martandam bhaskaram bhagam || 24 ||

indram vishnum harim hamsamarkam lokagurum vibhum |
trinetram tryaksharam tryangam trimurtim trigatim shubham || 25 ||

shanmukhaya namo nityam trinetraya namo namah |
chaturvimshatipadaya namo dvadashapanine || 26 ||

namaste bhutapataye lokanam pataye namah |
devanam pataye nityam varnanam pataye namah || 27 ||

tvam brahma tvam jagannatho rudrastva cha prajapatih |
tvam somastva tathadityastvamomkaraka eva hi || 28 ||

brihaspatirbudhastva hi tvam shukrastva vibhavasuh |
yamastva varunastva hi namaste kashyapatmajaha || 29 ||

tvayata tatamidam sarvam jagat sthavarajangamam |
tvatta eva samutpannam sadevasuramanusham || 30 ||

brahma cha aham cha rudrashcha samutpanna jagatpate |
kalpadau tu pura deva sthitaye jagato anagha || 31 ||

namaste vedarupaya ahrupaya vai namah |
namaste jnanarupaya yajnaya cha namo namah || 32 ||

prasidhasmasu devisha bhutesha kiranajnjvala |
samsararnavamagnanam prasadam kura gopate |
vedantaya namo nityam namo yajñakalaya cha || 33 ||
