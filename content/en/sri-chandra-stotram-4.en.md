Dhyana -
shvetaambaraanvitapurvaru-shubhavarannam |
shvetaashvayuktarathagam surasevitaamghrim || 1 ||
    dorbhyaam dhrutaabhayagadam varadam sudhaamshum
shrivatsamauktikadharan pranamaami chandram || 1 ||

aagneyabhaage saratho dashaashva-
-shchaatreyajo yaamunadeshajashcha || 2 ||
    pratyangmukhashtha caturasrapeethe
gadaadhaarango vararohiniishah || 2 ||

chandram caturbhujam devam keyuuramakutojjvalam |
    vaasudevasya nayanam shankarasya ca bhushanam || 3 ||

chandram ca dvibhujam gneyam shvetavastradharan vibhum |
    shvetamaalyaambaradharan shvetagandhaanulepanam || 4 ||

shvetchattradharan devam sarvaabharanabhushitam |
    etat stotram pathitvaa tu sarvasampatkaram shubham || 5 ||

Phalashrutihi -
kshayaapasmaarakushthaadi taapajvaranivaaranam |
    idam nishaakarastotram yah pahet satatam narah || 6 ||
    sopadravaat pramucyeta naatra kaaryaa vichaaraanaa || 6 ||
