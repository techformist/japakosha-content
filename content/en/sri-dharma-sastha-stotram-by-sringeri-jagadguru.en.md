jagatpratishthaaheturyah dharmah shrutyantakiirtitah |
    tasyaapi shaastaa yo devastamadaa samupaashraye || 1 ||

shrishankaaryaairhi shivaavatairaai
dharmapraachaaraaya samastakaale || 2 ||
    susthaapitam shringamahiidhravaryai
peetham yatiindraa paribhuushayanti || 2 ||

teshieva karmandivareshu vidyaa-
-tapodhaneshu prathitaanubhaavah || 3 ||
    vidyaasutiirtho'bhino'dyoogii
shaastaaraamlokayitum pratasthe || 3 ||

dharmasya goptaa yatipungavo'yam
dharmasya shaastaaramavaikshateti || 4 ||
    yuktam tadetadyubhayostayohi
sammelanam lokahitaaya noonam || 4 ||

kaale'smin kalimaladuushteh'pi dharmah
shrauto'yam na khalu vilopamaapa tatra || 5 ||
    hetuh Khalvayamiha noonameva naa'nyah
shaasta'ste sakalajanaikavandyapaadah || 5 ||

jnaanam shadaasyavarataatakripaikalabhyam
moksastu taarkshyavaravaahadayaiikalabhyah || 6 ||
    jnaanam ca moksha ubhayam tu vinaa shramena
praapyaam janaihi hariharatmajasatprasaadaat || 6 ||

yamaniyamaadisametai yatacittai yogibhi sadaa dhyeyam |
    shaastaam hridi kalaye dhaataram sarvalokasya || 7 ||

shabaragirinivaasah sarvalokaikapoojyah
natajanasukakaarii namrahrttaapaahaarii || 8 ||
    tridaditijasevyah svargamokshapradaa taa
hariharasutadevah santatam shaman tanotu || 8 ||
