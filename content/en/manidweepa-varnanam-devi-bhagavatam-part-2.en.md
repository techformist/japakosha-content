vyAsa uvAcha |
    puShparAga-MayAdagre kuNkumAruNavigrahaH |
    padma-rAga-mayaH sAlo madhye bhUshaiva tAdrashI || 1||

dashayo-jana-vAnairghye gopura-dvAra-saM yutaH |
    tanmaNi-stambha-saM yuktA maNDapAH shatasho nRupa || 2||

madhye bhavi samAsInAshchatuH-ShaShTimitAH kalAH |
    nAnAyudha-dharA vIrA ratna-bhUShaNa-bhUShitAH || 3||

pratyeka-loka-stAsAM tu tatalloka-sya nAyakAH |
    samantAtpadma-rAgasya parivAryA sthitAH sadA || 4||

svasva-loka-janaiHjuShTasva- sva-vAhanahetibhiH |
    tAsAM nAmAni vakShyAmi ShRuNtuvaM janamejaya || 5||

pingalAkhI vishAlAkhI sam-RiddhirvRddhireva cha |
    shraddhA svAhA svadhAbhikhyA mAyAsaMjvA vasundharA || 6||

triloka-dhAtrI sAtrI gAyatrI tridaShe-varI |
    sUrUpA bahurUpA cha skandamAtA'chyutapriyA || 7||

vimalA cha AmalA tadaruNI punarAruNI |
    prakRitir-vi-kRitirmiH sRiShTiH stihiriH saMhRutireva cha || 8||

sandhyA mAtA satI haMsee mardikA vajrikA parA |
    devamAtA bhagavatI devakI kamalAsanA || 9||

trimukhI saptamukhanyA surAsura-vimardinI |
    lamboshThI chordhvakeShI cha bahushIrShA vRukodarI || 10||

ratharekhAhvayA pashChAshchshirekhA tathAparA |
    gagana-vegA pavana-vegA chaiva tataH paramaH || 11||

agre bhavana-pAlA syAttatpashChAnmadanAturA |
    anaMgA-naMgamathanA tathaivAnaNgamekhalA || 12||

anaMgakusumA pashChAdvishvarUpA surAdikA |
    kShayaMkarI bhavaichChaktir-AkShobhyA cha tataH paramaH || 13||

satyAvAdinyatha prAktA bahurUpA shuchivrAtA |
    udArA-khyA cha vAgeshee chatuH-ShaShTitimAtA smRitAH || 14||

jvala-jjihvAnanAH sarvA vamaMtyo vahnimulbaNam |
    jalaM pibAmah sakalaM saMharAmo vibhAnasum || 15||

pavanaM staMbhayAmo'dya bhakShayAmo'khilaM jagataH |
    iti vAchaM sagirante kroDhasaMraklAchanAH || 16||

chApabANa-dharAH sarvA yuddhAyAivotsukAH sadA |
    daMShTra-kaTakaTArAvaiH badhIrI-katadiNmukhAH || 17||

piNgordhvekShyaH saM proktAshchApabANa-karAH sadA |
    shatAkShauhini-ikA senApyaikasyAM prakIrtitA || 18||

ekaikashakuteH sAmarthyaM lakShabrahmANDanAshane |
    shatAkShauhini-ikA senA tAdrashI nRupa-sattama || 19||

kiM na kuryAj-jagatasminnaShakyam vaktuMeva tataH |
    sarvApi yuddhasAmAgrI tasmInsAle sthitA mune || 20||

rathAnAM gaNanA nAsti hayAnAM kariNAM tathA

-shAtrANAM gaNanA tadvad-gaNAnAM gaNanA tathA || 21||

padma-rAga-MayAdagre gomeda-maṇinirmitaH |
    dashayo-jana-dairghyeNa prAkAro vartate mahAn || 22||

bhAsvaja-jja-prasU-nAbho madhya-bhUstasya tAdrashI |
    gomeda-kalpitAnyeva tadvAsi-sadanAni cha || 23||

pakShiNaH stambha-varyAshcha vRikShAH vApyaH sarAṁsi cha |
    gomeda-kalpitA eva kuNkumAruNavigrahAH || 24||

tanmadhyasthA mahAdevyo dvAtriMshachchhaktayaH smRitAH |
    nAnAshastra-praharaNA gomeda-maNibhUShitAH || 25||

pratyeka-loka-vAsinyaH parivArya samantataH |
    gomedasAle saM naddhA pishAchavadanA nRupa || 26||

svarloka-vAsibhirnityaM pUjitAshchakrabAhavaH |
    krodra-ktaIkShabhiMdhi pachachiMdhi daheti cha || 27||

vadanti satataM vAchaM yuddhotsuka-hRudam-tarAH |
    ekaikasyA mahAshak-terdashAkShauhini-ikA matA || 28||

senA tatrApyaikashaktiH lakShabrahmANDanAshinI |
    tAdrashINAm mahAsenA varNanIyA kathaM nRupa || 29||

rathAnAM naiva gaNAnA vAhAnAnAM tathaiva cha |
    sarvayuddhasamArambhaH tatra devyA virA-jate || 30||

tAsAM nAmAni vakShyAmi pApanAshakaraNA cha |
    vidyAhrIpuShTayaH prajnA sinevalI kUHustathA || 31||

rudrA vIryA prabhA nandA poShiNI ṛiddhiNA shubhA |
    kAlarAtrir- mahArAtrir- bhadrakAlI kapardinI || 32||

vikRitir- daNDiMuṇinyaH seṇḍukhaṇḍA shikhaṇḍiNI |
    niShumbha-shumbha-mathanI mahiShAsura-mardinI || 33||

iṇdrANI chaiva rudrANI shankArArdha-sharIrNee |
    nArI nArAyaNI chaiva tRishUlinyapi palinee || 34||

ambikA hlAdeenI pashChAdityevaM shaktayaH smRitAH |
    yadyeTAH kupitA devyastadA brahmANDanAshanaM || 35||

parAjayo naichAsAM kadAchitkvachidasti hi |
    gomeda-MayAdagre sadvajramaṇinirmitaH || 36||

dashayo-jana-tuMgo'sau gopura-dvAra-saM yutaH |
    kApATa-shRuNKhAla-baddho navavRukShasamujjhvalaH || 37||

sAla-stanmadhya-bhUyAdi sarvaM hIraMayaM smRutamaM |
    gRuhANee vIthayo rathyA mahAmArgANgANi cha || 38||

vRikShAlAvalAtaravaH sArangA api tAdrashAH |
    dIrghikAshreNayO vApya-stadAgAH kUpa-saM yutAH || 39||

tatra shrIbhuvaneShvaryA vasanti parichArikAH |
    ekaikA lakShadAsIbhiH sevita madagaravitAH || 40||

tAlavRuntadhArAH kAshchichchhaShakaDyakaram-bujAH |
    kAshchittAmbUla-pAtrANi dhArayantyo'tigarvitAH || 41||

kAshchittachChatradhAriNiyAshchAmaraNAmaM vidArikAH |
    nAnAvasttradharAH kAshchitkAshchitpUShpakaram-bujAH || 42||

nAnAdarshakarAH kAshchitkAshchitkuNmuma-lepanamaM |
    dhArayantyaH kajjalaM cha sIndUrachashakaM parAH || 43||

kAshchichchitramakatRi-yAHS pAdasaMvahane ratAH |
    kAshchittu bhUShAkAriNyo nAnAbhUShAdhArAH parAH || 44||

pUShpabhUShaNanimatRi-yAHS pUShpashRUṅgArikArikAH |
    nAnAvilAsa-chaturA bahavya evaM vidhAH parAH || 45||

nibaddha-paridhAnIyAH yuvatyaH sakalA api |
    devIkRupAlesha-vashtu-chChIkRitajagattrayAH || 46||

eTA dUtyaH smRutA devyaH shRUṅgAramadagarvitAH |
    tAsAM nAmAni vakShyAmi ShRuNe me nRupa-sattama || 47||

anaMgArUpA prathamApyanaMgamadanA parA |
    tRitIyA tu tataH prAktA sundarI madanAturA || 48||

tato bhavana-vegA syAttathA bhavanApalikA |
    syAstarvashi-shirANga-vadananAnnamekhalA || 49||

vidyud-dAmasamAnAM-aMgyaH kvaNAt-kaMcIguNAnvitAH |
    raṇamanjIracharaNA bahiramtari-tatataH || 50||

dhAvamAnAstu shobhante sarvA vidyul-latopamAH |
    kuBalAH sarvakAryeShu vetra-hastAH samantataH || 51||

aShTadikShu tathaItAsAM prAkArAdbahiH eva cha |
    sadanAni virAjante nAnAvAhanahetibhiH || 52||

vajrasAlAdagrabhAge sAlo vaidUryanirmitaH |
    dashayo-jana-tuMgo'sau gopura-dvAra-bhUShitaH || 53||

vaidUryabhUmiH sarvApi gRuhANi vividhAni cha |
    vIthyo rathyA mahAmArgAH sarve vaidUryanirmitAH || 54||

vApIkUpa-tadAgAshcha sravaNtINAM taTAni cha |
    vAlukA chaiva sarvApi vaidUryamaṇinirmitA || 55||

tatraShTadikShu parito brAhmyAdInAM cha maNDalamaM |
    nijaigaNaiH parivRRitaM bhArAjate nRupa-sattama || 56||

pratibrahmANDAmAtUNAm tAH saMashTayA IrutAH |
    brAhmI mAheShvarI chaiva kAumarI vaiShNavI tathA || 57||

vArAhI cha tathEndrANI chaAMuṇDaaSapta mAtaraH |
    aShTamI tu mahAlakShmIrnAmnA prAktAHstu mAtaraH || 58||

brahmarudrAdidevAnAM samAkArAstu tAH smRitAH |
    jagatkalyANakAriNyaH svasvasenAsamAvRRitAH || 59||

tatsAlasya chaturdvAreShu vahAni maheshituH |
    sajjAni nRupate santi sAlaṅkArANi nityaShahaH || 60||

dantiNaH koTIsho vAhAH koTIshu shibikAstathA |
    haMsaH siMhAshcha garudA mayUrA vRiShAbAstathA || 61||

tairyuktAH syandanAstadvata- koTIsho nRupanandana |
    pARShi-grAhasamAyuktA dhvajairAkAshachuM-binaH || 62||

koTIshastu vimAnAni nAnAchihnAnvitAni cha |
    nAnAvAditrayuktAni mahAdhvajayutAni cha || 63||

vaidUryamaṇisAlasyApyagre sAlo paraH smRutaH |
    dashayo-jana-tuMgo'sAvaindranIlAshma-ṇinirmitaH || 64||

tanmadhya-bhUstatheevIthyo mahAmArgA gRuhANi cha |
    vApIkUpa-tadAgAshcha sarve tanmaṇinirmitAH || 65||

tatra padmaM tu saM proktaM bahuyo-jaNavistRutamaM |
    ShoDashAraM dIpyamAnaM sudarshanamivAparamaM || 66||

tatra ShoDashashaktInAM sthAnAni vividhAni cha |
    sarvopaskara-yuktAni samRiddhAni vasanti hi || 67||

tAsAM nAmAni vakShyAmi ShRuNe me nRupa-sattama |
    karAlI vikarAlI cha tathomA cha sarasvatI || 68||

shrI dUrgoShA tathA lakShmI shrutehchaiva smRitirdhRutiH |
    shraddhA medhA matiH kAnti rAyA ShoDashashaktayaH || 69||

nIla-jImUta-saM kAshA karavAlakara-ambujAH |
    samAH kheTaka-dhAriNyo yuddhopakRantA-manasAH || 70||

senAnyash sakalA eTAH shrIdevyA jagadIshituH |
    pratibrahmANDasaM sthAnAM shaktInAM nAyikAH smRitAH || 71||

brahmANDa-kShobhakAriNi devI shakti-upabRihitAH |
    nAnAratha-samArUDhA nAnAshaktibhiranvitAH || 72||

etatapA-kraM vaktuM sahasrAsyopi na kShamaH |
    indranIla-mahAsAlAdagre tu bahu-visitRutaH || 73||

muktAprAkAro udito dashayo-jana-dairghyavAn |
    madhya-bhUHpUrvapRoktA tanmadhyeyaShTa-da-lAmbujamaH || 74||

muktAmaṇigaN-AkIraNaM vistRutaM tu sake- saramaM |
    tatra devIsamAkArA devyAyuddha-dharAH sadA || 75||

saM proktA aShTamantRiNyashcha jagadavA-taprabodhikAH |
    devIsamAnabhogAstA iM-giMjAnAstu paṇḍitAH || 76||

kuBalAH sarvakAryeShu svAmikAra-parAyaN AH |
    devyAbhiprAyabodhyastAshchaturA atisundarAH || 77||

nAnAshaktisamAyuktAH pratibrahmANDa-vartinAM |
    prANInAM tAH samAcAraM jnAnashaktyA vidanti cha || 78||

tAsAM nAmAni vakShyAmi mattaH ShRu nRupautama |
    anaMgakusumA prAktApi-anaNgakusumat urA || 79||

anaMgamadanaM tadvad-anaNgamadanAturA |
    bhavanA palA gagana-vegA chaiva tataH paramaH || 80||

shashirekhA cha gagana-rekhA chaiva tataH paramaH |
    pAshAMku-shavarAbhItidhArA A ruṇavigraH || 81||

vishvasaMbandhinI vArtAM bodhayanti pRatikShanamaM |
    muktAsAlAdagrabhAge mahAmArakato'da-bharaH || 82||

sAlauttamaH samuddiShTa dashayo-jana-dairghyavAn |
    nAnAsaubhAgya-saM yukto nAnAbhogasama nvitaH || 83||

madhya-bhUstAdrashI prAktA sadanAni tathaiva cha |
    ShaTkOna-matra vistIrNaM koNasthA devatAH shRuiNiHuH || 84||

pUrvakoNe chaturvaktro gAyatrIsahito vidhiH |
    kuṇḍikAkShaguṇAbhItidaṇḍAyuddhAdhAraH paraH || 85||

tadAyuddha-dharA devI gAyatrI paradevatA |
    vedAH sarve mUrtimantaH shAstrANi vividhAni cha || 86||

smRutayaH cha purANAni mUrtimanti vasanti hi |
    ye brahmavigrahAH santi gAyatrIvigrahAsha ye || 87||

vyAhRutInAM vigrahAsha ste nityaM tatra santi hi |
    rakShakoNe shankhachakragadAmbujakarAmbujA || 88||

sAvitrI vartate tatra mahAviShNuH cha tAdrashaH |
    ye viShNu vigrahAH santi matsya kUrmAdayo'khilAH || 89||

sAvitrI vigrahA ye cha te sarve tatra santi hi |
    vAyukoNe parashva-kShamAlAbhayavarAnvitaH || 90||

mahArudro vartate'tra sarasvatyapi tAdrashI |
    ye ye tu rudrabhedAH syurdakShiNAsyAdayo nRupa || 91||

gaurIbhedAshcha ye sarve te tatra nivasanti hi |
    chatuH-ShaShTyAgAmA ye cha ye chAnyA'pyAgAmAH smRutAH || 92||

te sarve mUrtimantashcha tatraiva nivasanti hi |
