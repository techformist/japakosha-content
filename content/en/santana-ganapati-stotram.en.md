namo' Astu gananaathaaya siddhibuddhiyutaaya ca |
sarvapradaaya devaaya putravriddhipradaaya ca || 1 ||

guurodaaraaya gurve goptre guhyaasitaaya te |
gopyaaya gopitaasheshabhuvanaaya chidaatmane || 2 ||

vishvamoolaaya bhavyaaya vishvasrutikaraaya te |
namo namaste satyaaya satyaapoorNaaya shundine || 3 ||

ekadantaaya shuddhaaya sumukhaaya namo namaH |
prapaNajanapaalaaya pranaataartivinaashine || 4 ||

araNam bhava deviisha santatiim sudRuDaa kuru |
bhaviShyanti cha ye putraa mattkulie gaNanaayaka || 5 ||

te sarve tava puujaarthaM nirataaH syurvaromataH |
putrapradamidaM stotraM sarvasiddhipradaayakam || 6 ||

## English Translation

(1) Salutations to Lord of the Ganas, endowed with accomplishment and wisdom,
Giver of all benefits to the god, bestower of progeny and growth.

(2) To the vast-bellied guru, protector of hidden secrets,
To the mysterious one who pervades all the worlds, to the conscious self.

(3) Salutations again and again to the source of the universe, the sublime one,
Creator of the universe, to the truthful one, the complete one, to the trunked Lord.

(4) Salutations to the single-tusked pure one, the pleasant-faced one,
Protector of surrendered people, destroyer of the afflictions of the surrendered.

(5) O Lord of gods, grant refuge; make the lineage strong,
O Lord of Ganas, may the sons who will be born in my lineage be engaged in your worship.

(6) May they all always be engaged from limb to limb in your worship,
This hymn bestowing progeny grants all accomplishments.
