|| shrii-mahā-lakṣmyai namaḥ ||

shubhaṃ karoti kalyāṇaṃ karoti |
śreyaḥ pradōṣīvātu śobhate mukhya-mantrāṣu |
kāntiṃ vikāśyati ca kānti-sampanneṣu |
jagat-prasūtē jayati jaya-kāra-vāditrī ||

lakṣmīṁ nāmāmi nityaṃ loka-hitaiṣiṇīm |
sureśvarīṃ sarasija-nilaye bhajāmahe |
devīṃ praṇatārta-harīṃ prasanna-vadanām |
prātaḥ-kāle dhyāyet-devīṃ sarva-siddhi-pradāyinīm ||

padmāsane padma-varṇe padma-netre |
padma-haste śubhade sarva-bhūta-priye |
padmāyatākṣi lakṣmī namāmi tvāṃ |
pāhi māṃ pāpa-hare devi mama hṛdi nivāsaya ||

kamalākṣi kamalā devī kāmeśvarī |
kamalāsanā kāntā kāma-pradāyinī |
kāntā-aravinda-mukhī kāntā-vidyā-dhare |
kānti-pūrṇā kānti-dā kāntim-antara-rūpiṇī ||

gaurī-varṇe guṇaiḥ pūrṇā goviṇdapriye |
goviṇda-patnī goviṇda-vallabhe gomate |
goviṇda-vallabhe goviṇda-priya-sakhī |
goviṇda-manoharī goviṇdapriye ||

lakṣmīṃ lakṣaṇa-sampannāṃ lakṣmī-patiṃ |
lakṣmī-nivāsinīṃ lakṣmī-īśvarīm |
lakṣmīṃ lakṣmī-sahodarīṃ lakṣmī-priyām |
lakṣmīṃ lakṣmī-sutāṃ lakṣmī-kula-priyām ||

śrī-vidyāṃ śrī-pradāṃ devīṃ śrī-śaktim |
śrī-mahā-lakṣmīṃ śrī-saubhāgya-dāyinīm |
śrī-śrīḥ pradāyinīṃ śrī-mantra-pūjitām |
śrī-devīṃ sarva-mantraiḥ śrī-yutāṃ śrīḥ ||

saubhāgya-dāyinīṃ devīṃ sarva-saubhāgya-vardhinīm |
saumyāṃ saumya-vadanāṃ devīṃ saubhāgya-sampradāyinīm |
sarva-maṅgala-maṅgalye śiva-svarūpe |
namāmi bhakti-dāyinīṃ nitya-pūjitām ||

|| shrii mahā-lakṣmī suprabhātaṃ sampūrṇam ||

## English Translation

(1) She who does auspicious deeds, she who bestows welfare,
    Who shines brightly in the principal mantras,
    Who spreads radiance among those filled with splendor,
    The mother of the world, who is victorious with the sound of victory.

(2) I bow to Goddess Lakshmi always, who is the well-wisher of the world,
    The ruler of gods, who resides in the lotus, we worship her.
    O Goddess who removes the afflictions of devotees, who has a pleasing face,
    One should meditate on the Goddess in the morning hours, O bestower of all accomplishments.

(3) O Lakshmi with lotus-like eyes, who sits on a lotus seat,
    Who is lotus-colored, who has lotus eyes, who holds a lotus in her hand,
    O lotus-eyed one, I bow to You.
    O Goddess who destroys sins, please protect me and dwell in my heart.

(4) O Lotus-eyed Goddess, O Kamala, O Goddess of desire,
    Who sits on a lotus, who is beautiful, who grants desires,
    Who has a beautiful face like a blooming lotus,
    Who holds all knowledge, O complete in radiance, O bestower of radiance,
    O embodiment of the mantras of beauty.

(5) O fair-complexioned one, filled with all virtues,
    Beloved of Govinda (Lord Vishnu), wife of Govinda,
    Delight of Govinda, O Mother of wealth,
    Dear friend of Govinda, O one who pleases Govinda.

(6) I bow to Lakshmi, complete with all auspicious marks,
    The consort of Lakshmi (Lord Vishnu), who resides with Lakshmi,
    The ruler of Lakshmi, the sister of Lakshmi,
    Dear to Lakshmi, the daughter of Lakshmi, beloved to the family of Lakshmi.

(7) O Goddess who embodies the knowledge of Sri, who bestows prosperity,
    O Sri Mahalakshmi, who gives all good fortune,
    O bestower of wealth, worshipped through sacred mantras,
    O Goddess Sri, worshipped by all mantras, filled with wealth.

(8) O Goddess who bestows good fortune, who increases all auspiciousness,
    O gentle one with a pleasing face, O Goddess who grants all blessings,
    O embodiment of all that is auspicious, O one who is the form of auspiciousness,
    I bow to the bestower of devotion, who is worshipped always.

|| Sri Mahalakshmi Suprabhata is complete ||
